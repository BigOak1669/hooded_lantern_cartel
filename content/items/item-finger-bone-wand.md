# Item: Finger-bone wand

## Summary

A wand shaped like an oversized finger bone, held by Tornar during the attempted animation of Mara and other prepared dead.

## Observed Properties

- Necrotic energy flowed from it into the coffin and wrapped bodies.
- The effort left Tornar coughing black ichor and weakened him.
- Gradrick took it from Tornar after Sab ended the undead activity.

## Current Status

Held by Gradrick. Its name, construction, limits, and further effects are unknown. “Wand of Unlife” is a descriptive working label, not a confirmed identification.
