# Item: Belt of the Thornswild / Gold-and-Unicorn-Horn Buckle

## Summary

- Likely target item from [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]]' belt-buckle lead, recovered from the elven warrior statue in the [[location-third-vakish-mound|third Vakish mound]].

## Current Status

- Recovered and worn by [[pc-grond|Grond]] at the end of [[2026_0503 Session 11]].
- Identified by [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]] in Session 12 as the Belt of the Thornswild.
- Kept by the party, with [[pc-grond|Grond]] as holder/wearer, after the party returned Vakish's earlier protective cloak instead.

## Known Facts

- Vakish described the desired object as solid gold and inlaid with unicorn horn.
- The belt or buckle was found on the waist of a wounded elven warrior statue in a sculptural battle chamber.
- When Grond removed it, the statue's pale sword light went dark and three lizardfolk statues animated.
- When Grond wore the belt, it magically fit him and granted +4 Strength while worn.
- Vakish connected it to the lost Thornswild elven people/region.

## What The Players Know

- It matches Vakish's description closely enough to be the likely quest objective.
- It has at least one observed magical effect: +4 Strength while worn by Grond.
- In Session 12, the party chose to keep the belt and return Vakish's borrowed cloak.

## Owner / Holder

- [[pc-grond|Grond]].
