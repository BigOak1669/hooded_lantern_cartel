# Item: Cobra-Headed Staff

## Summary

A fine dark-wood staff topped by a carved cobra head, recovered from a lectern between two lizardfolk statues in the [[location-broken-sigil-mound|broken-sigil mound]].

## Observed Properties

- Removing it caused several small snakes to emerge.
- When [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] pointed it at the snakes, they shifted direction but continued following the party.
- It may influence or attract snakes, but full control is not established.

## Current Status

Held by Oogie and awaiting identification, likely from [[npc-mazzah|Mazah]].
