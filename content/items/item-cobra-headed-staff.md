# Item: Staff of the Cobra

## Summary

A fine dark-wood +1 staff topped by a carved cobra head, recovered from a lectern between two lizardfolk statues in the [[location-broken-sigil-mound|broken-sigil mound]].

## Observed Properties

- Removing it caused several small snakes to emerge.
- When [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] pointed it at the snakes, they shifted direction but continued following the party.
- [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]] established that [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] can throw it down once per day to transform it into a large serpent servant.
- Snakes regard Oogie as friendly.
- When Oogie accidentally touched the marked mound's door, the painted serpent treated him as friendly and slipped away rather than attacking.

## Current Status

Identified by Mazzah and held by Oogie.
