# NPC: Finn

## Summary

- Lantern-bearer and guide hired by the party for Barrow Mounds expeditions, useful under pressure but transactional enough to make his cut and head for town once the job was done.

## Current Status

- Alive in or around [[location-helix|Helix]]. After helping get Oogie back from the barrows in Session 07, Finn took his negotiated 8-gold share and separated from the party again.
- In Session 11 recap/table talk, Finn was explicitly not with the party and was not available for this delve.

## Known Facts

- Finn has repeatedly served as a lantern-bearer, torchbearer, and guide for the party's Barrow Mounds expeditions.
- He warned the party about the bronze gate and the lower cryptist on earlier delves.
- During Session 07 he helped carry dead [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] out of danger and back toward [[location-helix|Helix]].
- He directed the party to the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] to liquidate their loot after the escape.
- He pushed to receive his negotiated share, collected 8 gold, and then sprinted off toward the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]].
- He reads as useful, practical, and hireable, but not deeply bound to the party beyond paid work.
- Session 11 reinforces that Finn should not be recorded as an active hireling for the third Vakish mound delve.

## What The Players Know

- Finn is a competent enough expedition hireling and local guide.
- He can be relied on for light, route guidance, and basic survival help during barrow trips.
- He is also very much a paid hand: once his share is due, he expects to be paid and can part ways quickly.
- The party now has direct evidence that he remained helpful during a crisis retreat, not just routine exploration.

## Relationships

- Hireling and guide for the party during multiple Barrow Mounds expeditions.
- Assisted [[pc-oogie|Oogie]]'s emergency recovery by helping get his body back toward Helix.
- Familiar with the party's Helix-facing support loop, including the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] and [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]].
