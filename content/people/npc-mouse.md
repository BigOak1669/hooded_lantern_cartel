# Mouse

Mouse is a skinny Helix courier in greasy leathers and a low skullcap. He appears deaf or nearly deaf and communicates primarily through signs, often with his companion Dennis interpreting.

## Current Role

Mouse carries food and drink twice daily to the large occupant of the serpent-and-skull marked mound. He uses a concealed knock—two taps, a pause, then one tap on a side stone—and passes the delivery through a narrowly opened door.

Bolo recognized him and noted his unusually free spending. Dennis said the mound smells like a bad butcher's shop. The party paid toward an extra delivery and arranged for Bolo to strengthen the recipient's ale. Mouse and Dennis completed that delivery in Session 15 and left before the party entered.

Mouse's employer, knowledge, and source of money remain unknown. He is distinct from [[npc-mox|Mox]], the young torchbearer.
