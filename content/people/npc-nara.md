# Nara

Nara was an undead household maid who continued sweeping and maintaining the entrance hall of the serpent-and-skull marked mound. Her apron carried her name, and she wore a ring of iron keys.

## What the Party Encountered

- Worked slowly in a faded dress and apron while the party entered.
- Asked the visitors to wipe their boots and responded to Oogie's rapport-spore communication.
- Allowed the party to take her keys, though she would not formally hand them over.
- Retained household routines despite her undead condition.

## Current Status

Sab's Turn Undead destroyed Nara, leaving ash where she had stood. The party did not learn how long she had been dead or who she served before Tornar occupied the mound.
