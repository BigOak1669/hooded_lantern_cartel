# NPC: Tiramel

## Summary

- Red-haired elven spellcaster from the Iron Chain fallout, rescued from the northern barrow and left as the most sympathetic surviving witness to Nabu's betrayal and Ikram's death.

## Current Status

- Alive after Session 05. Last known to be back in Helix's orbit, grieving Ikram, caring for the rescued hound, and separated from the party after the camp confrontation.

## Known Facts

- Tiramel entered the northern barrow with Ikram, Nabu, and Perry while pursuing Werner's sledgehammer.
- Tiramel and Ikram were trapped behind a barred door after the expedition went bad.
- Once rescued, Tiramel was more composed and pragmatic than Ikram and quickly explained the betrayal story to the party.
- Tiramel gave the party a fine dagger in thanks, which later proved to be the returning dagger Sab kept.
- During the next session's fallout, Tiramel traveled with the party, attempted holy magic against the undead hirelings, and helped reason through Werner and Nabu's likely movements.
- Tiramel stayed back while the party approached the camp, then later received Ikram's token and the news of his death through Tacey.
- Tiramel appears to have taken charge of the rescued dog.

## What The Players Know

- Tiramel is one of the clearest non-hostile survivors of the Iron Chain mess.
- Tiramel has direct knowledge of Nabu's betrayal and of the northern-barrow expedition that went wrong.
- Tiramel's goodwill toward the party is genuine enough to have included a gift, cooperation, and trust after Ikram's death.

## Relationships

- Companion of [[npc-ikram|Ikram]] and fellow victim of Nabu's betrayal.
- Former party-mate of [[npc-nabo|Nabu]] and Perry within the Iron Chain expedition.
- Cooperative ally of the party after the northern barrow rescue.
- Original source of the [[item-returning-dagger|returning dagger]] now carried by Sab.

## Session History

- 2026-01-25 [[2026_0125 Session 04]] — Freed with Ikram from the barred room, identified Nabu as the likely betrayer, and gave the party a fine dagger in thanks.
- 2026-02-08 [[2026_0208 Session 05]] — Traveled with the party after the barrow, tried holy magic against the undead, stayed back during the camp approach, and later received Ikram's token and death news.
