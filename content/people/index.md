# People

- **[Alistair](npc-alistair)** — Street eccentric in Helix associated with shouted clues about the chalice.
- **[Arch / Archie](npc-arch-archie)** — Infernal/liminal figure tied to prior Archie ticket events. This session Oogie heard the devil's laugh in the back of his mind when approaching the chaotic tablet chamber.
- **[Bartholomew](npc-bartholomew)** — Fair Church brother who received the party in crisis, guided Oogie's restoration setup, and helped make the church feel like a practical refuge rather than a formal temple.
- **[Billsworth](npc-billsworth)** — Merchant wagon driver and supplier who brought fine Blackbark gin and a starshine eel into Helix, helped seed the Session 08 feast, and later had his cart hit during the predawn raid.
- **[Billworth Turgin](npc-billworth-turgin)** — Older merchant wagon driver hauling metal goods to Helix who shared road rumors with the party.
- **[Bolo](npc-bolo)** — Brazen Strumpet proprietor/barkeep who welcomed the party back late at night and offered leftover stew.
- **[Celestia](npc-celestia)** — Quest-giver tied to the chalice recovery, discussed only in recap.
- **[Finn](npc-finn)** — Lantern-bearer and guide hired by the party for Barrow Mounds expeditions, useful under pressure but transactional enough to make his cut and head for town once the job was done.
- **[Gant](npc-gant)** — Gant, who brought up the Mort hook.
- **[Harlan](npc-harlan)** — Young man killed before the session, likely the ghoul victim found on the road to Helix.
- **[Ikram](npc-ikram)** — Human Iron Chain fighter freed from the northern barrow who briefly aligned with the party before being murdered by Nabu during the camp confrontation outside Helix.
- **[Locked and Loaded](npc-locked-and-loaded)** — Tower owner or facilitator tied to the dungeon run and the party's entry point.
- **[Mazzah](npc-mazzah)** — Wizard outside/in Helix whose tower is larger inside than outside and protected mainly by increasingly explicit 'do not disturb' signs plus a repeating illusion. He returned the party's owl and identified Sab's cloak as a Cloak of Elvenkind
- **[Mort](npc-mort)** — Abrasive half-orc Fair Church worker whose memorable contribution to Oogie's restoration scene was dumping foul refuse out of a kitchen window onto the compost-ground setup.
- **[Mox](npc-mox)** — Mox is a human boy, roughly thirteen or fourteen, whom the party hired at the Brazen Strumpet as a torchbearer for a Barrow Mounds expedition.
- **[Nabo](npc-nabo)** — Goblin member of the Iron Chain who was suspected of betrayal and later served as an accomplice to Werner. Nabo was killed by Werner after being wounded by the party.
- **[Nora](npc-nora)** — Temple attendant or local guide in the settlement reached through the portal.
- **[Osric](npc-osric)** — Curio dealer who negotiated aggressively and appears tied to the Silver Goblet thread.
- **[Perry](npc-perry)** — Halfling member of the Iron Chain. His body was found in the eastern chamber of the northern barrow; confirmed deceased.
- **[Ravos](npc-ravos)** — Mentioned Fair Church healer or priest contact identified by Gant as the person to seek for real healing help.
- **[Rusk](npc-rusk)** — Jittery local hireling who claimed to know the correct barrow but failed to become the party's guide.
- **[Sira Vey](npc-sira-vey)** — Traveling tattoo artist who came into Helix with Billsworth's wagon, tattooed Oogie and Sab during the Session 08 celebration, and shared a little road-lore about Orr, Sand and Sea, and a distant southeastern site.
- **[Steel Bone cultist](npc-steel-bone-cultist)** — Lowest visible field operatives of the Steel Bone Brotherhood, appearing as red-robed, steel-bone-masked cultists who shadowed the party around the Barrow Mounds and the Helix retreat route.
- **[Tacey](npc-tacey)** — Brazen Strumpet staff member seen cleaning up when the party returns late.
- **[Tiramel](npc-tiramel)** — Red-haired elven spellcaster from the Iron Chain fallout, rescued from the northern barrow and left as the most sympathetic surviving witness to Nabu's betrayal and Ikram's death.
- **[Unnamed bovine deity figure](npc-unnamed-bovine-deity-figure)** — Sacred figure or shrine presence in the dungeon that received offerings.
- **[Vakish](npc-vakish)** — Duplicate placeholder. Use Vakish Baharus as the canonical NPC note.
- **[Vakish Baharus](npc-vakish-baharus)** — Rose Quartz proprietor, jeweler-curio buyer, and new Helix merchant contact who buys fancy grave goods and feeds the party pointed barrow leads.
- **[Werner](npc-werner)** — Werner: to be questioned at first light.
