# Tornar

Tornar was a gaunt black-robed ritualist or wizard working in the western rooms of the serpent-and-skull marked mound. He held a wand shaped like an oversized finger bone and promised Mort that he could bring Mort's wife Mara back.

## Session 15

Tornar performed an animation rite over Mara's body and other prepared dead. Necrotic energy flowed from the wand, but the effort caused him to cough black ichor and collapse to a knee. After Sab's Turn Undead ended the undead activity, Gradrick took the wand from Tornar. Orlin then transformed into a bear and killed him.

## Evidence and Uncertainty

The party recovered journals and scraps from Tornar's rooms. They contain incoherent references to a serpent, a tablet described as inheritance rather than treasure, and a devouring ram. The exact names, theology, faction relationships, and meaning of the notes remain unresolved. The party did not establish whether Tornar belonged to the Steel Bone Brotherhood.
