# NPC: Mort

## Summary

- Abrasive half-orc Fair Church worker whose memorable contribution to Oogie's restoration scene was dumping foul refuse out of a kitchen window onto the compost-ground setup.

## Current Status

- Alive at the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] in [[location-helix|Helix]], present as a church-side worker or novice but not currently established as a trusted healer.

## Known Facts

- Mort was present during the party's emergency stop at the Fair Church after the Session 06 disaster.
- He appeared at or near the kitchen window during Oogie's resurrection setup.
- He dumped refuse onto or near the compost-pile scene and complained about Oogie's corpse.
- His behavior immediately marked him as unpleasant compared with the more helpful Bartholomew and Gant.
- Gant explicitly recommended [[npc-ravos|Ravos]] rather than Mort if the party wanted competent healing help.

## What The Players Know

- Mort works at the Fair Church, but he is not the church contact the party is most likely to trust.
- He comes across as unsanitary, abrasive, and a poor first choice for serious care.
- The party's clearer useful church contacts are [[npc-bartholomew|Bartholomew]] and [[npc-gant|Gant]], with [[npc-ravos|Ravos]] named as the better healer.

## Relationships

- Shares church context with [[npc-bartholomew|Bartholomew]] and [[npc-gant|Gant]] at the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]].
- Implicitly contrasted with [[npc-ravos|Ravos]] as the less competent healing option.

## Session History

- 2026-03-08 [[2026_0308 Session 07]] — Interrupted Oogie's restoration setup by dumping refuse from the kitchen window and reinforced his reputation as the least appealing Fair Church helper.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- His exact formal role is still loose: novice, worker, or low-ranking church helper all fit better than a confident priest/healer label.
- No evidence yet shows that Mort actually performed healing competently on-screen.
