# NPC: Vakish Baharus

## Summary

- Rose Quartz proprietor, jeweler-curio buyer, and new Helix merchant contact who buys fancy grave goods and feeds the party pointed barrow leads.

## Current Status

- Alive in [[location-helix|Helix]] and actively open for more business at the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]].
- In Session 11, he was not encountered in person, but the party found the likely [[item-hero-belt-gold-and-unicorn-horn-buckle|belt/buckle]] from his three-mound lead.
- In Session 12, the party returned to Vakish after finishing the third mound. He accepted the second gold jackal, bought the diamond rings and ornate urn, recognized the recovered belt as the [[item-hero-belt-gold-and-unicorn-horn-buckle|Belt of the Thornswild]], and let the party keep it in exchange for returning his earlier protective cloak.

## Known Facts

- Vakish runs the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]], a Helix shop focused on jewelry, curios, and saleable tomb loot.
- He identified the four amber vials from the sealed barrow as perfume rather than potions.
- He bought the party's [[item-gold-ring|gold ring]] and [[item-four-ornate-amber-vials|four ornate amber vials]] for a total of 60 gold.
- He did not want the now-nonmagical chalice as ordinary dishware.
- He offered the party a new treasure lead involving a fancy belt buckle believed to be in one of three barrows southeast of the obelisk.
- He appears interested in recurring business from barrow delvers and is willing to buy future fancy grave goods.
- The party was still pursuing his southeastern-barrow lead in Session 10, including returning to the Barrow Mounds after resupply in Helix.
- By Session 11, the party had reached the third marked mound and recovered a gold-and-unicorn-horn hero belt/buckle matching his description, but had not yet returned it to him.
- In Session 12, he identified the belt as the Belt of the Thornswild and explained that the Thornswild was a lost elven forest/tribal region north and east of the mounds.
- He named the castle shown by Oogie's fading tablet-map as [[location-castle-zentolin|Castle Zentolin]], on a clear lake beyond the fog.
- He said the fog arrived roughly 50-60 years ago, contact with the Thornswild was lost, traders who tried to continue through the fog did not return, and the fog now appears to be easing day by day.

## What The Players Know

- Vakish is a useful Helix fence/buyer for unusual treasure.
- He can appraise at least some tomb loot competently.
- He is motivated by profit but also supplies actionable leads that may be worth following.
- His belt-buckle lead is one of the clearest next expedition hooks to come out of Session 07.
- As of Session 11, the item appears found but not delivered; the open decision is whether the party returns it, negotiates over it, or keeps using it.

## Relationships

- Proprietor of the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]].
- Merchant contact for the party's Helix-facing loot cycle.
- Now tied to the open thread around the unicorn-horn-inlaid belt buckle in the southeastern barrows.
- Connected to the party's new Castle Zentolin and Thornswild leads through his larger map and lore.
