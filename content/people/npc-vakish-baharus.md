# Vakish Baharus

Vakish Baharus owns the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]], where he appraises and buys jewelry, curios, and saleable grave goods. He is profit-minded but has repeatedly given the party useful information rather than merely taking their loot.

## Business with the Party

- Identified the four amber vials as perfume and bought them with a gold ring.
- Supplied the three-mound lead that produced two gold jackals and the Belt of the Thornswild.
- Bought the diamond rings and ornate urn recovered from the third mound.
- Let Grond keep the Belt of the Thornswild in exchange for the return of Vakish's protective cloak.

## Regional Knowledge

Vakish recognized the Thornswild connection and named the castle on Oogie's fading map as [[location-castle-zentolin|Castle Zentolin]]. He described the fog as cutting off travel and contact with the elven tribes of [[location-thornswild|Thornswild]], while also observing that it now appears to be easing.

His account opens a wider regional direction; it does not establish what happened inside the fog.
