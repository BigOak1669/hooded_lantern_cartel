# NPC: Sira Vey

## Summary

- Traveling tattoo artist who came into Helix with Billsworth's wagon, tattooed Oogie and Sab during the Session 08 celebration, and shared a little road-lore about Orr, Sand and Sea, and a distant southeastern site.

## Current Status

- Alive and apparently still traveling with Billsworth after the Session 08 feast night.

## Known Facts

- Works as a tattoo artist using specialized implements.
- Traveled into Helix with Billsworth's cargo wagon.
- Tattooed Oogie with a large iridescent anchor chest tattoo.
- Tattooed Sab with two raven shoulder tattoos.
- Wants to recharge her implements in Orr.
- Comes from a place called the City of Sand and Sea.

## What The Players Know

- Dry, competent, travel-worn tattoo artist who fit naturally into the Brazen Strumpet celebration.
- Comfortable taking coin, reading people, and telling selective road stories.
- Carries broader travel knowledge than most Helix locals, though some of it is still only hearsay.

## Relationships

- Travels with [[npc-billsworth|Billsworth]].
- Worked the Session 08 feast-night crowd at [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]].
- Tattooed [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] and [[pc-sab|Sab]].

## Session History

- 2026-03-22 [[2026_0322 Session 08]] — Arrived with Billsworth's wagon, joined the feast atmosphere at the Brazen Strumpet, accepted Oogie's Trance, tattooed Oogie and Sab, and shared some of her background and travel lore.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- Her name is rendered as both `Sira Vey` and `Syrah Vey`; this note keeps `Sira Vey` as the canonical id for now while preserving the alternate spelling in aliases.
- Her southeastern ruin / pyramid rumor is still hearsay and should not yet be treated as grounded location canon.
