# NPC: Bartholomew

## Summary

- Fair Church brother who received the party in crisis, guided Oogie's restoration setup, and helped make the church feel like a practical refuge rather than a formal temple.

## Current Status

- Alive at the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] in [[location-helix|Helix]], functioning as one of the church's main approachable intake and support figures.

## Known Facts

- Bartholomew was present when the party brought Oogie's body to the Fair Church after the Session 06 disaster.
- He helped direct the party to the cemetery compost pile as the best place to restore a fungal person.
- He accepted donations during the church-side intake and restoration scene.
- He offered the party basic shelter and helped establish the church as a place for food, rest, and practical aid.
- He appears to work closely with [[npc-gant|Gant]] and to tolerate or manage disruptions from [[npc-mort|Mort]].

## What The Players Know

- Bartholomew is one of the clearest friendly faces at the Fair Church.
- He is more welcoming than Mort and helps make the church legible as a useful Helix support site.
- The party knows him as someone who can receive people in trouble, accept offerings, and help route them to the church's more suitable resources.

## Relationships

- Works with [[npc-gant|Gant]] at the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]].
- Shares church context with [[npc-mort|Mort]], whose behavior he implicitly has to smooth over.
- Connected to [[npc-ravos|Ravos]] through the church's broader healing/refuge network.

## Session History

- 2026-03-08 [[2026_0308 Session 07]] — Received the party at the Fair Church, directed Oogie's restoration setup, accepted donations, and offered basic shelter.
