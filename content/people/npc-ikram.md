# NPC: Ikram

## Summary

- Human Iron Chain fighter freed from the northern barrow who briefly aligned with the party before being murdered by Nabu during the camp confrontation outside Helix.

## Current Status

- Dead. Nabu slit his throat during the Session 05 standoff before the party could rescue him.

## Known Facts

- Ikram entered the northern barrow with Tiramel, Nabo, and Perry while chasing Werner's sledgehammer and the chalice lead.
- He and Tiramel were trapped behind a barred door after Nabo's betrayal.
- Once freed, he became a temporary ally of convenience with the party while they pursued Nabo back toward Helix.
- He accompanied the party to the camp outside Helix, where Nabu took him hostage to force a disarm.
- After the party refused to surrender, Nabu killed him immediately.
- The party recovered Ikram's coin and identifying chain links and arranged for his token to be passed back to Tiramel.

## What The Players Know

- Ikram was not an enemy by the time the party met him; he had been betrayed by his own group and wanted revenge on Nabo.
- His death sharpened the fallout inside the Iron Chain and made Tiramel's survival more emotionally significant.
- His name was transcript-noisy, but the vault is standardizing on Ikram.

## Relationships

- Companion of [[npc-tiramel|Tiramel]].
- Member of the Iron Chain expedition that also included Nabo and Perry.
- Temporary ally of the party after being freed from the barrow.
- Murdered by [[npc-nabo|Nabo]] during the camp standoff.

## Session History

- 2026-01-25 [[2026_0125 Session 04]] — Freed with Tiramel from the barred room in the northern barrow and identified Nabo as the likely betrayer.
- 2026-02-08 [[2026_0208 Session 05]] — Traveled with the party, was taken hostage at camp, and was killed when Nabu slit his throat.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- Source spellings vary widely, including Ekram, Ekrum, Ekam, and Incam. The canonical vault form is Ikram.
