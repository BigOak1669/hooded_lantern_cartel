# Campaign Timeline

This timeline tracks the campaign's major changes, not every room, purchase, or rumor.

## Ore and Furnace

- **2025-11-16 — [[2025_1116 Session 02|Session 02]]:** The party escaped the underworld dungeon through a portal, entered Ore and Furnace, and accepted Celestia's commission to acquire a silver goblet near Helix.

## Establishing Helix

- **2026-01-11 — [[2026_0111 Session 03|Session 03]]:** Harlan's death led the party to Tacey, Werner, Mazzah, and the goblet-and-sledgehammer mystery in Helix.
- **2026-01-25 — [[2026_0125 Session 04|Session 04]]:** The first organized Barrow Mounds expedition freed Ikram and Tiramel and exposed Nabo's betrayal of the Iron Chain.
- **2026-02-08 — [[2026_0208 Session 05|Session 05]]:** Ikram, Nabo, and Werner died at the camp outside Helix; the party recovered Werner's hammer, a bag of holding, and the mound-north-of-the-obelisk note.
- **2026-02-22 — [[2026_0222 Session 06|Session 06]]:** A red-robed watcher drew the party toward a prominent barrow, where marked skeletons and a trap brought Oogie down.
- **2026-03-08 — [[2026_0308 Session 07|Session 07]]:** Oogie returned through an Archie ticket at the Fair Church, Dern joined the party, and Vakish supplied the three-mound belt lead.
- **2026-03-22 — [[2026_0322 Session 08|Session 08]]:** The great Helix feast deepened the party's local ties; Gant warned Sab about Mort before a coordinated predawn raid.

## The Vakish Mounds and Thornswild

- **2026-04-05 — [[2026_0405 Session 09|Session 09]]:** The party recovered the Owl of Magnus, gold jackals, gems, and an Alarm scroll from the first Vakish mound.
- **2026-04-19 — [[2026_0419 Session 10|Session 10]]:** A flooded tomb yielded the Charm Person scroll, and the party opened the third Vakish mound.
- **2026-05-03 — [[2026_0503 Session 11|Session 11]]:** Grond claimed the hero belt and the party defeated the animated lizardfolk battle tableau.
- **2026-05-17 — [[2026_0517 Session 12|Session 12]]:** The party recovered Thornswild lore and a fading map toward Castle Zentolin, then discovered the serpent-and-skull marked mound.

## The Marked Mound

- **2026-05-31 — [[2026_0531 Session 13|Session 13]]:** The party removed the cobra-headed staff from the Broken-Sigil Mound and retreated from its snakes.
- **2026-06-28 — [[2026_0628 Session 14|Session 14]]:** Owl surveillance exposed Mouse's deliveries to the marked mound; Gant reported Mort and a corpse missing, and the party prepared a fortified delivery before returning.
- **2026-07-12 — [[2026_0712 Session 15|Session 15]]:** The party entered the marked mound, confirmed Mort and Mara's identities, ended Tornar's attempted animation, killed Tornar, and recovered the Hollow Gate writ naming Quasqueton.
