# Location: Barred Western Room

## Summary

- Side room behind the barred western door where the party found and freed Ikram and Tiramel of the Iron Chain.

## Description

- Western side room off the entry chamber, initially closed behind a barred door.
- Quiet argument from inside gave away that living survivors were trapped there rather than undead or empty silence.
- Became the rescue site that clarified the Iron Chain betrayal inside the northern barrow.

## Current Relevance

- Canonical note for the rescue room tied to Ikram, Tiramel, Nabo's betrayal, and the pursuit of Werner's sledgehammer.

## What The Players Know

- Sab and Orlin heard a man and woman arguing quietly through the barred door before it was opened.
- Once the door was opened, the party freed Ikram and Tiramel the Red.
- The two survivors said they had entered seeking Werner's sledgehammer and believed Nabo trapped them, killed or abandoned Perry, and escaped with the hammer.
- Tiramel handed over a fine-looking dagger from the barrow as thanks after the rescue.
