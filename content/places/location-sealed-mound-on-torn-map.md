# Location: Sealed Mound on Torn Map

## Summary

- Unopened sealed mound identified on the party's torn map, forming the earliest concrete barrow lead tied to Werner's sledgehammer and chalice rumors.

## Description

- Exterior mound marked as sealed on the recovered scrap map from Session 03.
- Revisited with Finn in Session 04, when the party confirmed it would require forcing entry and treated it as one of the nearby candidate mounds tied to the chalice trail.
- Important because it anchors the original torn-map clue even though the party has still not opened it.

## Current Relevance

- Still a live but unresolved exterior lead in the Barrow Mounds because it links the first recovered map clue to the later mound-field audit.

## What The Players Know

- In Session 03, the party recovered a crude map to a `sealed burial mound` and concluded it likely matched Werner's directions.
- Werner's lost sledgehammer and the chalice rumor initially made this sealed mound seem like the likeliest target.
- In Session 04, Finn identified the sealed mound on the scrap map but narrowed the likely chalice source to one of two nearby mounds rather than confirming this mound as the exact site.
- The mound remains unopened, so all current understanding of it is inferential and map-based rather than explored firsthand.
