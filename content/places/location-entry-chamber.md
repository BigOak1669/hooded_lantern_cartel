# Location: Entry Chamber

## Summary

- Central first room inside the freshly disturbed northern barrow, linking the trapped western room and the more open eastern chamber.

## Description

- Initial chamber reached after entering the northern barrow in Session 04.
- Includes the barred western door and the opening into the eastern chamber.
- Shows signs that other explorers had already passed through before the party arrived.

## Current Relevance

- Canonical room-level note for the Session 04 northern-barrow interior split, now moved under the nested Barrowmaze branch.

## What The Players Know

- The party entered this chamber after choosing the fresher northern mound instead of the older southern candidate.
- From here, Sab and Orlin checked the barred western door and heard a man and woman arguing quietly on the far side.
- The room also gave access to the eastern chamber where the mummy, fresh corpse, and gray ooze hazard were discovered.
- It functioned as the staging point for both the ooze fight and the rescue of the trapped Iron Chain survivors.
