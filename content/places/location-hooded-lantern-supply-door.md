# Location: Hooded Lantern supply door

## Summary

- Recurring strange door / basic supply shop tied to the Hooded Lantern cartel and the broader Locked and Loaded home-office layer.

## Description

- Appears as a familiar storefront or door that ordinary locals may not notice or acknowledge.
- Opens onto a sparse, practical shop where party members can buy basic supplies.
- In Session 10, the door appeared near the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] in [[location-helix|Helix]], separate from the mundane [[location-helix|Helix]] storefront where Sab bought oil.
- The Session 10 shopkeeper was a friendly rock gnome who looked "like a pile of stones" and welcomed members of the Hooded Lantern cartel.

## Current Relevance

- Gives the party a recurring, portable supply access point connected to the long-running home-office / Locked and Loaded continuity.
- The Helix appearance may be temporary, interdimensional, extra-temporal, or otherwise not part of ordinary town geography.

## What The Players Know

- The party recognized the Session 10 door as reminiscent of a strange supply place they had seen earlier in the City of Ore and Furnace.
- Ordinary townsfolk do not necessarily perceive or acknowledge the door.
- The rock gnome shopkeeper recognized Oogie and Orlin as people with torn [[item-archie-ticket|Archie tickets]] and warned them to be careful because they do not know how many times a ticket can really be torn.
- The shop appears connected to the Hooded Lantern cartel and the broader Locked and Loaded home-office thread.
