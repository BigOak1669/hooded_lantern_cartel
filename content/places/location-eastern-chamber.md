# Location: Eastern Chamber

## Summary

- Open side chamber in the northern barrow containing a standing sarcophagus, a mummy, a fresh corpse, and the gray ooze hazard.

## Description

- Eastern chamber reached off the entry chamber during Session 04.
- Contains a standing sarcophagus with a mummy and a fresher corpse nearby.
- Became immediately dangerous when the gray ooze animated near the corpse and attacked.

## Current Relevance

- Canonical room-level hazard note for the northern-barrow split, preserving the gray-ooze fight and the strongest evidence about Perry's likely fate.

## What The Players Know

- The chamber held both an older preserved body in the standing sarcophagus and a much fresher corpse.
- The fresh corpse proved dangerous by proximity to a gray ooze.
- The ooze showed corrosive effects in combat before the party killed it.
- Tiramel later identified the belongings in the chamber as Perry's, making it likely that the corpse was Perry.
