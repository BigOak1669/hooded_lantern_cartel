# Location: Tablet chamber

## Summary

- Chamber in the flooded ceremonial barrow containing a pedestal with an ornate urn/amphora and a chaotic tablet. Reading the tablet caused it to turn to ash and briefly reveal a map of the Barrow Mounds and a castle beyond the explored area. The chamber also had a submerged spike-pit trap.

## Description

- The chamber lay beyond a swollen ironbound door. Its central pedestal held the urn and tablet. The floor concealed a trap that dropped Grond into a spike pit under the water.

## Current Relevance

- The chamber's physical rewards were removed or spent, but its map clue now drives the Castle Zentolin and chaotic-tablet threads.

## What The Players Know

- Oogie read the tablet, which transformed into ash and a fading map.
- Oogie and Grond tried to preserve the map from memory.
- Grond triggered the spike pit, took 4 damage, escaped, and was healed by Sab.
