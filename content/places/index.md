# Places

- **[Already-open barrow](location-already-open-barrow)** — A previously opened mound containing a disturbed skeleton and a gold ring but no major remaining treasure.
- **[Barred Western Room](location-barred-western-room)** — Side room behind the barred western door where the party found and freed Ikram and Tiramel of the Iron Chain.
- **[Barrow Mounds](location-barrow-mounds)** — Barrow Mounds: The party traveled to the first northern candidate mound and explored its inner crypt and altar room.
- **[Barrow Mounds exterior near the obelisk](location-barrow-mounds-exterior-near-the-obelisk)** — Redirect/helper note for a Session 12 exterior movement beat near the obelisk.
- **[Billsworth's cart](location-billsworth-s-cart)** — Billsworth's cart: The party helped unload casks of Blackbark gin and a starshine eel from the cart.
- **[Brazen Strumpet](location-brazen-strumpet)** — Inn/tavern where the party returned late, ate leftover stew, slept, and was charged room and board.
- **[Broken-Sigil Mound](location-broken-sigil-mound)** — A previously opened barrow whose outer door bears a broken painted sigil. The party explored it in Session 13.
- **[camp outside Helix](location-camp-outside-helix)** — Grounded Session 05 holdout note for a temporary camp outside Helix, preserved flat because it is an aftermath site rather than a settled part of the current canonical tree.
- **[Castle Zentolin](location-castle-zentolin)** — Legendary castle seen in Oogie's fading tablet-map vision and later named by Vakish. It sits on a crystal-clear lake beyond/east of the Barrow Mounds and has been cut off from contact by the fog.
- **[Dungeon / underworld complex](location-dungeon-underworld-complex)** — Hazardous delve containing blob undead, shrine rooms, loot, and a portal exit.
- **[Eastern Chamber](location-eastern-chamber)** — Open side chamber in the northern barrow containing a standing sarcophagus, a mummy, a fresh corpse, and the gray ooze hazard.
- **[Entry Chamber](location-entry-chamber)** — Central first room inside the freshly disturbed northern barrow, linking the trapped western room and the more open eastern chamber.
- **[Fair Church](location-fair-church)** — Practical church-and-refuge site in Helix where Oogie was restored on the cemetery compost grounds after the Session 06 disaster.
- **[Flooded ceremonial barrow](location-flooded-ceremonial-barrow)** — Use Third Vakish Mound for this location.
- **[Helix](location-helix)** — Party returned here at night, rested at the Brazen Strumpet, sold loot at the Rose Quartz the next morning, resupplied, and retrieved the owl from Mazah.
- **[Hooded Lantern supply door](location-hooded-lantern-supply-door)** — Recurring strange door / basic supply shop tied to the Hooded Lantern cartel and the broader Locked and Loaded home-office layer.
- **[Interstitial bright room / afterlife space](location-interstitial-bright-room-afterlife-space)** — Bright liminal in-between place where Oogie and Dern encountered a handsome devil between death and return.
- **[Locked and Loaded Tower](location-locked-and-loaded-tower)** — Staging point for the dungeon run and source of the ticketed entry.
- **[Lower chamber beneath the prominent barrow](location-lower-chamber-beneath-the-prominent-barrow)** — An underground chamber below the prominent mound showing frescoes, graffiti, blood, bones, incense, and trapped passages.
- **[Maza's Tower](location-maza-s-tower)** — Legacy spelling-variant pointer note for Mazah's Tower, the canonical Helix wizard-tower note.
- **[Mazah's Tower](location-mazahs-tower)** — Wizard's tower with many 'do not disturb' signs and an illusion repeating the warning. The party retrieved the owl and had the cloak identified there.
- **[mound north of the obelisk](location-mound-north-of-the-obelisk)** — Specific mound named in Werner's note and later marked by Mazzah as the place tied to the chalice lead.
- **[Northern Barrow](location-northern-barrow)** — Flat umbrella / review note for the freshly disturbed northern barrow now that the Session 04 interior rooms have moved into the canonical `northern-barrow` Barrowmaze branch.
- **[Obelisk area](location-obelisk-area)** — A landmark southeast of the explored mounds and a likely reference point for the note about a tomb north of the obelisk.
- **[Ore and Furnace](location-ore-and-furnace)** — Older settlement hub anchoring the campaign before the move to Helix: source of the portal-linked temple district, the party's loot sale, and Celestia's chalice job.
- **[Portal chamber](location-portal-chamber)** — Room used by the party as an escape route out of the dungeon.
- **[Prominent barrow](location-prominent-barrow)** — Legacy simplified pointer note for Prominent Barrow with Standing Stones and Shaft, the canonical exterior survivor.
- **[Prominent barrow with lower chamber and trap system](location-prominent-barrow-with-lower-chamber-and-trap-system)** — Prominent barrow with lower chamber and trap system
- **[Prominent Barrow with Standing Stones and Shaft](location-prominent-barrow-with-standing-stones-and-shaft)** — Distinctive exterior mound marked by standing stones and a shaft rig, serving as the surface entrance to the dangerous lower chamber explored in Session 06 and the crisis retreat point in Session 07.
- **[Road from Ore and Furnace to Helix](location-road-ore-and-furnace-to-helix)** — Main overland route the party used to reach Helix; site of the meeting with Billworth and the dog diversion.
- **[Rose Quartz](location-rose-quartz)** — Vakish Baharus's jewelry/curio shop in Helix. The party turned in a gold jackal, sold rings and an urn, discussed the Thornswild and Castle Zentolin, and finalized keeping the Belt of the Thornswild.
- **[Sealed barrow with nested casket](location-sealed-barrow-with-nested-casket)** — Sealed barrow with nested casket
- **[Sealed burial mound](location-sealed-burial-mound)** — Legacy Session 03 wording for Sealed Mound on Torn Map, the canonical survivor for this torn-map landmark.
- **[Sealed Mound on Torn Map](location-sealed-mound-on-torn-map)** — Unopened sealed mound identified on the party's torn map, forming the earliest concrete barrow lead tied to Werner's sledgehammer and chalice rumors.
- **[Serpent-skull marked mound](location-serpent-skull-marked-mound)** — Smaller mound entered at night by a large lone figure. Its door had fresh paint showing a black serpent curling around a skull, with candle/incense light or warmth behind it. The party chose not to enter.
- **[Shrine room with bovine deity figure](location-shrine-room-with-bovine-deity-figure)** — Shrine space where the party made offerings and recovered a giant gold disc/coin.
- **[Southern Candidate Barrow](location-southern-candidate-barrow)** — Nearby mound whose broken entrance appeared older and not recently trafficked.
- **[Swampy campsite off the road](location-swampy-campsite-off-the-road)** — Small campsite where Harlan was killed and the corpse-eating creature was found feeding.
- **[Tablet chamber](location-tablet-chamber)** — Chamber in the flooded ceremonial barrow containing a pedestal with an ornate urn/amphora and a chaotic tablet. Reading the tablet caused it to turn to ash and briefly reveal a map of the Barrow Mounds and a castle beyond the explored area.
- **[Temple District / temple settlement](location-temple-district-temple-settlement)** — Moonlit settlement reached through the portal, controlled or framed by temple authority.
- **[The Brazen Strumpet](location-the-brazen-strumpet)** — The Brazen Strumpet: The party spent their carousing money on a feast here and met Sira Vey.
- **[The old watchter](location-the-old-watchter)** — Acceptance/local-whisper holdout note for an off-mainline site said to have yielded the iron key, excluded from canonical migration unless stronger Session 02-07 continuity appears.
- **[The Rose Quartz](location-the-rose-quartz)** — The Rose Quartz: The party's base of operations, targeted during the raid.
- **[Third Vakish Mound](location-third-vakish-mound)** — The third of Vakish Baharus' three marked belt-buckle mounds in the Barrow Mounds.
- **[Thornswild](location-thornswild)** — A once-nearby elven forest or tribal region north and east of Helix/Barrow Mounds. Wade clarified that the fog caused the cutoff from Thornswild.
- **[Thornswild burial chamber](location-thornswild-burial-chamber)** — Quiet tomb chamber with a stone slab/catafalque, green burial shroud, and faded wall scenes of a hunter, lizardfolk raiders, and a wounded elven champion. The party recovered the word Thornswild from the writing here.
- **[Town shops / market](location-town-shops-market)** — Review-level generic commercial-area note from Session 02, preserved flat because the source material does not yet justify a more specific canonical sub-site.
- **[Water-filled barrow tomb](location-water-filled-barrow-tomb)** — Barrow Mounds tomb with cold standing water, rusty portcullises, water zombies, flooded sarcophagi, and the scroll tube later identified as Charm Person.
- **[World Overview](location-world-overview)** — High-level campaign region linking the older Ore and Furnace arc to the current Helix-and-Barrow-Mounds expedition loop.
