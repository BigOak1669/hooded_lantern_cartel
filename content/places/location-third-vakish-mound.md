# Location: Third Vakish Mound

## Summary

- The third of [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]]' three marked belt-buckle mounds in the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]].
- It is the newly opened mound at the end of [[2026_0419 Session 10]], then the main dungeon site of [[2026_0503 Session 11]] and [[2026_0517 Session 12]].
- This is the same site as the flooded ceremonial barrow.

## Description

- The mound was opened through a stone seal already cracked by [[pc-grond|Grond]], followed by a damp, swollen wooden door banded in black iron.
- A ten-foot stair led down into a sparse flooded ceremonial chamber with shallow black water, deeper pools, and hidden stone doors.
- One hidden swiveling door led to a funerary alcove with a half-submerged altar and ornate box of elven ash, bones, and rings.
- Another hidden door or passage was protected by a rancid-oil fire trap controlled by an underwater mechanism.
- A larger sculptural battle chamber depicted a wounded elven warrior statue with a pale glowing sword facing three lizardfolk statues. The hero belt was around the elf statue's waist.
- Additional Session 12 chambers included the [[location-thornswild-burial-chamber|Thornswild burial chamber]] and [[location-tablet-chamber|tablet chamber]].
- The burial chamber held faded Thornswild writing, elven champion/lizardfolk-raider imagery, and the green shroud later identified as a [[item-cloak-of-elvenkind|Cloak of Elvenkind]].
- The tablet chamber held an ornate urn/amphora, a chaotic tablet that became a fading map, and a submerged spike-pit trap.

## Current Relevance

- Mostly complete as a Vakish lead. The party recovered the belt, finished the nearby Thornswild/tablet chambers, returned to Helix, and settled the belt choice with Vakish.
- Still open only for any deeper northern water or other skipped side area the party intentionally left unexplored.
- The fire trap's exact final state after the Session 11 combat remains unresolved unless later play clarifies it.

## What The Players Know

- This is the third mound from Vakish's lead, not the older prominent barrow with the shaft.
- The mound contains hidden doors, a flooded chamber, an oil-fire trap, elven funerary remains, and a statue chamber linked to the belt.
- Removing the belt extinguished the pale sword light and animated three stone lizardfolk.
- The belt magically fit Grond and granted +4 Strength while worn.
- The mound also held Thornswild lore, the Cloak of Elvenkind, an ornate urn sold to Vakish, and the chaotic tablet-map clue pointing toward Castle Zentolin.
- The deep northern water remains unexplored.

## Important NPCs / Factions

- [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]] - source of the lead, not present in the mound.
- [[faction-thornswild-elven-tribes|Thornswild elven tribes]] - connected through tomb imagery, the belt, and the recovered Thornswild name.
