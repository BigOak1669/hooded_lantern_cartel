# Location: Fair Church

## Summary

- Practical church-and-refuge site in Helix where Oogie was restored on the cemetery compost grounds after the Session 06 disaster.

## Description

- Humble church and refuge in [[location-helix|Helix]] serving poor, sick, maimed, or spiritually adrift people.
- Includes a cemetery and compost grounds that the brothers judged appropriate for restoring Oogie's fungal body.
- Functions more as a practical shelter and healing stop than as an ornate or politically central temple.

## Current Relevance

- Confirmed emergency-aid site in Helix, with approachable clergy and a proven role in resurrection, food, and lodging support.

## What The Players Know

- The party chose the Fair Church over Mazah for immediate help after escaping the barrow with Oogie's body.
- Bartholomew and Gant directed the party to the compost pile as the best place to restore a fungal person.
- Mort works at the site and provided a memorable if unsanitary interruption from the kitchen window.
- Gant identified Ravos as the more capable healer if the party needs better medical help later.
- The church was also discussed as a place where the party could get free food and a bed.
