# Location: Water-filled barrow tomb

## Summary

- Barrow Mounds tomb with cold standing water, rusty portcullises, water zombies, flooded sarcophagi, and the scroll tube later identified as [[item-charm-person-spell-scroll|Charm Person]].

## Description

- A sealed mound/tomb in the Barrow Mounds that Grond forced open during Session 10.
- The entrance led down into cold knee-deep water, with rusty portcullises to the east and west.
- The west-side room held flooded sarcophagi and yielded the scroll tube later identified as [[item-charm-person-spell-scroll|Charm Person]].
- The east-side room was associated with the destroyed water zombies and putrid sludge, and the party chose not to search it.

## Current Relevance

- Partially explored. The water zombies were destroyed and the spell scroll tube was recovered, but the east-side sludgy room remains unsearched.

## What The Players Know

- Water zombies inhabited the tomb and were defeated.
- One rusty portcullis was broken open so the party could search the west-side sarcophagi.
- Grond found a spell scroll tube in a flooded sarcophagus; in Session 11 it was identified as [[item-charm-person-spell-scroll|Charm Person]].
- The east-side room looked unpleasant and possibly unrewarding enough that the party chose to leave it alone.

## Important NPCs / Factions

- None noted.
