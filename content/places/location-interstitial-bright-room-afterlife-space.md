# Location: Interstitial bright room / afterlife space

## Summary

- Bright liminal in-between place where Oogie and Dern encountered a handsome devil between death and return.

## Description

- Bright, mostly empty space experienced during the Archie-ticket resurrection flow rather than during ordinary travel.
- Occupied in Session 07 by a devil figure who spoke calmly, handled questions and tickets, and sent the dead back into the world.
- Functions less like a mapped room in Helix or the barrows and more like a temporary transition zone between death and return.

## Current Relevance

- First clearly grounded location note for the campaign's Archie-ticket afterlife logic.
- Ties directly to Oogie's death-and-return sequence, Dern's introduction, and the Tablet of Chaos lead named by the devil.

## What The Players Know

- Oogie arrived here after dying in the lower chamber beneath the prominent barrow.
- Dern also appeared here after dying in his own backstory shipwreck vignette.
- A handsome devil figure confirmed they were dead or between states, gave Dern a ticket, allowed Oogie one question, and then sent both back.
- Oogie asked where the most powerful item in the Barrow Mounds was, and the devil named the Tablet of Chaos while qualifying that power depends on the wielder.
- Both returned physically outside the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]], which suggests the space connects back into the living world but is not itself mapped inside Helix.

## Hierarchy / Attached Sites

- Parent area: none confirmed; this is a liminal or afterlife-adjacent space rather than a settled physical region.
- Classification: transient liminal space / resurrection waypoint.
- Child / attached sites: none confirmed.
- Related / sibling sites: [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] as the Session 07 return point, and [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] as the physical resurrection setup site used by the living party members.

## Important NPCs / Factions

- Handsome devil figure
- Dern
- Oogie

## Session History

- 2026-03-08 [[2026_0308 Session 07]] — Oogie and Dern met the devil here between death and return, received answers and tickets, and then dropped back into the world outside the Rose Quartz.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- It is still unclear whether this space is infernal, bureaucratic, divine, Archie-specific, or part of some broader liminal system.
- The devil's answer about the Tablet of Chaos may be truthful, misleading, or context-dependent.
- The location may not be stable or repeatable in exactly the same form for every resurrection event.
