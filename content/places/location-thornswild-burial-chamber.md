# Location: Thornswild burial chamber

## Summary

- Quiet tomb chamber with a stone slab/catafalque, green burial shroud, and faded wall scenes of a hunter, lizardfolk raiders, and a wounded elven champion. The party recovered the word Thornswild from the writing here.

## Description

- A more solemn chamber inside the flooded ceremonial barrow. Sab's respectful funerary prayer preceded the shroud becoming or revealing itself as a magical cloak.

## Current Relevance

- Source of the Cloak of Elvenkind and a key piece of Thornswild lore.

## What The Players Know

- The buried figure was likely an elven warrior or champion connected to Thornswild.
- The wall scenes suggest conflict with lizardfolk raiders, but they are not a complete historical account.
- Sab recovered the green shroud/cloak after a funerary rite.
