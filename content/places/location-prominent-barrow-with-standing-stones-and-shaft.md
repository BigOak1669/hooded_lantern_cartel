# Location: Prominent Barrow with Standing Stones and Shaft

## Summary

- Distinctive exterior mound marked by standing stones and a shaft rig, serving as the surface entrance to the dangerous lower chamber explored in Session 06 and the crisis retreat point in Session 07.

## Description

- More visually distinctive barrow than the ordinary mounds, with standing stones and a tripod or block-and-tackle arrangement over the shaft.
- Acts as the exterior shell and access point for the deeper [[location-lower-chamber-beneath-the-prominent-barrow|lower chamber beneath the prominent barrow]].
- By Session 07, this mound had become a clearly recognized danger site associated with the recent disaster, emergency retreat, and repeated Steel Bone imagery at the edge of the mist.

## Current Relevance

- One of the most dangerous currently grounded barrow sites, because it combines a known lower-level dungeon, trapped passages, marked skeletons, and apparent Brotherhood pressure or projection.

## What The Players Know

- The mound is easy to distinguish from simpler barrows because of its standing stones and shaft setup.
- In Session 06, the party descended from here into a lower chamber showing blood, incense, bones, graffiti, and signs of regular traffic.
- Oogie was downed below this mound during the Session 06 disaster, forcing the party into a retreat.
- In Session 07, the group climbed back out with Oogie's body while red-robed steel-bone-masked figures appeared around the mound at the edge of the lantern light.
- Those red-robed figures behaved more like illusions, projections, or staged intimidation than immediate attackers, though the exact explanation is still unresolved.
