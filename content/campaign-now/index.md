# Campaign Now

## Previously On

The Hooded Lantern Cartel has used Helix as its base while exploring the Barrow Mounds, recovering strange grave goods and following signs of organized activity among the tombs. Their most persistent concern is the Steel Bone Brotherhood: masked figures, undead former hirelings, cryptic notes, and a freshly marked mound have repeatedly placed the Brotherhood near the party's path without fully revealing its purpose.

## Where Things Stand

The party is back in Helix after retrieving a magical cobra-headed staff from a partially explored mound. Grond kept the Belt of the Thornswild, Oogie carries the now-identified Staff of the Cobra, and Mazzah is holding a dangerous dark-arts spellbook recovered from another tomb. The party has not yet committed to leaving Helix or the Barrow Mounds, although Castle Zentolin and the wider region remain possible future destinations.

## Threads in Motion

The immediate focus is the serpent-and-skull marked mound. Gradrick's owl surveillance showed Mouse delivering supplies to a large half-orc-like occupant. Mort has disappeared from the Fair Church along with a corpse, making him the strongest—but still unconfirmed—candidate. The mound's imagery is associated with an evil serpent-and-death cult and may represent a deeper layer behind, within, or separate from the Steel Bone Brotherhood.

## Recent Discoveries

- Mouse supplies the marked mound with food and drink twice daily using a concealed knock signal.
- The Staff of the Cobra is a +1 weapon that can become a large serpent servant once per day; snakes regard Oogie as friendly.
- Mazzah connected the mound sigil to evil serpent, death, and underworld iconography.
- Mazzah traded Blind/Deafen and Hypnotize spellbooks to the party and secured the dangerous spellbook.
- The Fair Church is missing both Mort and a corpse.

## Current Regions and Destinations

- **Helix** remains the party's active base for lodging, rumors, magical help, religious contacts, and supplies.
- **Barrow Mounds** remain the immediate expedition region, especially the serpent-and-skull marked mound and the unfinished broken-sigil mound.
- **Castle Zentolin** is a known wider destination that the party has discussed but not yet pursued.

## Party Intent

The party arranged for Bolo to fortify the ale in Mouse's next delivery. They intend to observe the delivery through the owl, return to the marked mound, recover the owl, and investigate or confront the occupant if circumstances allow.
