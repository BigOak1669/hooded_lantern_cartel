# Thread: Thornswild lost history

## Summary

- The tomb, belt, and Vakish's lore establish Thornswild as an elven forest/tribal region lost behind the fog for a century or more.

## Why It Matters

- Thornswild ties together the recovered belt, the elven champion imagery in the flooded tomb, the lost eastern/northern region, and the fog around the Barrow Mounds.

## Current State

- Open lore thread. The party knows Thornswild was an elven forest or tribal region north/east of the Barrow Mounds and that the fog caused the cutoff from it. They do not yet know what happened there.

## Open Questions

- What happened to Thornswild and its elven people?
- How does the Belt of the Thornswild relate to the champion shown in the tomb?
- What happened to Thornswild after the fog cut it off?
- How is Thornswild connected to Castle Zentolin, lizardfolk raiders, or other regional forces?

## Next Likely Touchpoints

- Any lore follow-up about the belt, the elven champion, the fog, or Castle Zentolin.
- Travel north/east once routes through the fog become possible.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- The party has only historical fragments. Avoid treating the tomb imagery as a complete or objective account of Thornswild's fall.
