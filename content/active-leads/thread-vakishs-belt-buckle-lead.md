# Thread: Vakish's belt-buckle lead

## Summary

- Vakish Baharus offered the party a paid treasure lead involving a fancy belt buckle believed to be in one of three barrows southeast of the obelisk. The party found it, Vakish identified it as the [[item-hero-belt-gold-and-unicorn-horn-buckle|Belt of the Thornswild]], and the party kept it by returning Vakish's earlier protective cloak.

## Why It Matters

- This is one of the clearest concrete post-Session-07 expedition hooks: a named buyer, a specific treasure target, and a narrowed search area in the Barrow Mounds.

## Current State

- Resolved as a patron lead. In Session 12, Vakish recognized the belt and offered the party a choice between keeping it or keeping his earlier protective cloak. The party chose the belt, and [[pc-sab|Sab]] returned Vakish's cloak.
- [[pc-grond|Grond]] remains the known wearer/holder of the belt. Its full magical properties are still an item mystery, but the original Vakish lead no longer needs to stay open.

## Open Questions

- Does the belt have additional powers, drawbacks, or curses beyond the observed +4 Strength?
- Is Vakish's information reliable, partial, or bait for a higher-risk delve?
- What other grave goods or complications might be tied to the same mounds?

## Next Likely Touchpoints

- Future belt revelations belong on [[item-hero-belt-gold-and-unicorn-horn-buckle|Belt of the Thornswild]] or related Thornswild/Castle Zentolin threads.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- Raw Session 07 evidence gives more color about the buckle's ornamentation, but the core canonical thread is already strong without overcommitting to every flourish.
- The exact mound is now resolved as the [[location-third-vakish-mound|third Vakish mound]].
- Treat this as a paid treasure lead / buyer interest, not a formal debt or obligation to Vakish unless later play establishes one.
