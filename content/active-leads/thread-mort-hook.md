# Thread: Mort hook

## Summary

- Gant quietly warned Sab that Mort of the Fair Church may be tied to the Steel Bone Brotherhood and handed her a half-mask found under Mort's bed.

## Why It Matters

- This is the clearest new Helix-side continuity threat from Session 08.
- If the suspicion is well-founded, the Steel Bone Brotherhood may have reached into one of the party's safer local support networks.

## Current State

- Mort is under suspicion, not proven guilt.
- Sab currently holds the hidden half-mask and has not yet publicly blown the matter open.
- Gant wants discreet watchfulness rather than a loud accusation without stronger proof.

## Open Questions

- Is Mort actually tied to the Steel Bone Brotherhood, or only entangled with their materials or contacts?
- How long has Mort been slipping away, and where has he been going?
- When and how will Sab bring the rest of the party into the loop?
- Does Bartholomew already suspect something, or is Gant acting alone for now?

## Next Likely Touchpoints

- Quiet surveillance of Mort.
- Follow-up with Gant and possibly the Fair Church.
- Any later Helix incident that ties Brotherhood activity back into town.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- Mort's actual relationship to the Brotherhood is still unproven.
- The half-mask is strong evidence of danger, but not yet definitive proof of membership or intent.
