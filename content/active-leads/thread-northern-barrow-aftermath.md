# The Northern Barrow and Werner's Hammer

## Resolution

The search for Werner's lost sledgehammer began with the northern barrow and ended in blood at the camp outside Helix. The party freed Iron Chain survivors Ikram and Tiramel, learned that Nabo had betrayed them, and pursued the stolen hammer.

At the camp, Nabo killed Ikram during a hostage demand. Werner then killed Nabo, attacked the party, and was killed in turn. The party recovered the hammer, a bag of holding, and the note pointing toward the mound north of the obelisk.

## Lasting Consequences

- Ikram, Nabo, and Werner are dead.
- Tiramel survived and the party retained her trust strongly enough for Sab to keep the returning dagger.
- The hammer passed into Grond's orbit.
- The note and the undead near the mounds fed the larger Steel Bone Brotherhood investigation.
