# The Sealed Mound on the Torn Map

The torn map recovered on the road to Helix marked a sealed mound, but the party's early comparison with Finn's fuller map did not establish that it was exactly the same barrow tied to Werner's hammer and the silver goblet.

The lead remains a background possibility rather than an active plan. Its value is as an unvisited or not-fully-identified point in the wider Barrow Mounds, not as several separate open quests.
