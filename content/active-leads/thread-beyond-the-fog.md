# Beyond the Fog

## Where It Stands

The party's world is beginning to widen beyond Helix and the western Barrow Mounds. Oogie's fading tablet map showed more mounds and a castle beyond them. Vakish identified the castle as Castle Zentolin and described the fog as the reason contact and travel have been cut off.

Thornswild is part of the same horizon: the flooded ceremonial tomb preserved elven imagery, the name Thornswild, a magical cloak, and the belt Grond now wears. Vakish says the elven tribes of Thornswild have not been seen for a century or more.

The Session 15 Hollow Gate writ adds Quasqueton as another possible destination beyond the immediate mound work. The party has not established whether it is the same site as Castle Zentolin or another stronghold entirely.

## Why It Persists

The party has indicated interest in moving on as it grows in power, and teleportation may eventually change the practical shape of travel. This is therefore a durable regional direction even while a local Helix investigation occupies the immediate foreground.

## Open Questions

- What lies in the fog-shrouded reaches east of the explored mounds?
- What happened within Thornswild after contact was lost?
- What is Castle Zentolin's present condition?
- How reliable is the rough copy of the tablet map?

The established player-facing name is Castle Zentolin; alternate map spellings remain unresolved rather than silently substituted.
