# Mort and the Marked Mound

## Where It Stands

The party has connected several strong clues without yet proving the final identity of the mound's occupant:

- Gant found a Steel Bone half-mask beneath Mort's bed.
- Mort has disappeared from the Fair Church, and one corpse is missing.
- A large, half-orc-like figure entered a smaller mound marked with a freshly painted serpent around a skull.
- Gradrick's owl saw Mouse use a concealed knock and pass supplies to a large gray-green hand inside.
- Mouse says the mound smells like a bad butcher shop and that he makes deliveries twice a day.

## Immediate Plan

The party paid for an extra delivery, asked Bolo to fortify the recipient's sour ale, and intends to watch the delivery before returning to the mound.

## What Remains Uncertain

The evidence strongly suggests that Mort is the occupant, but the party has not seen his face there. The symbol may connect the site to the Steel Bone Brotherhood, but Mazzah's reading suggested a deeper serpent-and-death tradition rather than a simple confirmation.
