# Thread: Fog around the Barrow Mounds

## Summary

- The fog blocks or hides access east/north; traders who tried to continue through it after it arrived never returned. Wade clarified that the fog caused the cutoff from Castle Zentolin and Thornswild.

## Why It Matters

- The fog is now a campaign-scale travel barrier and clock. It explains why Castle Zentolin and Thornswild have been cut off, and its apparent lifting may open the next region of play.

## Current State

- Open. Vakish says the fog arrived roughly fifty or sixty years ago, blocked access east/north, and swallowed traders who tried to continue through it. Wade clarified that it caused the cutoff from Castle Zentolin and Thornswild. Vakish also says it seems to be easing day by day.
