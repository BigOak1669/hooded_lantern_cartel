# Encounter: Sculptural Battle Chamber Statues

## Summary

- Combat in the [[location-third-vakish-mound|third Vakish mound]] after removing the hero belt from the elven warrior statue animated three stone lizardfolk and coincided with a half-formed skeleton threat.

## Current Status

- Resolved. The party destroyed all immediate combat threats and survived.

## Participants

- [[pc-sab|Sab]]
- [[pc-oogie|Oogie]]
- [[pc-grond|Grond]]
- [[pc-orlin|Orlin]]
- [[pc-gradrick|Gradrick]]

## Location

- Sculptural battle chamber inside the [[location-third-vakish-mound|third Vakish mound]].

## Session History

- 2026-05-03 [[2026_0503 Session 11]] - Grond removed the hero belt, the pale sword light went dark, and the stone lizardfolk animated.

## Outcomes / Consequences

- One lizardfolk statue, called Razor Fang at the table, hit Grond for 1 damage.
- Sab destroyed one statue with a critical crossbow shot.
- Oogie dislodged the fire-trap wedge while moving during combat and later cast Rage on Orlin.
- Orlin crossed the fire, took 1 fire damage, transformed into a bear, received Rage, and destroyed the half-formed skeleton.
- Gradrick blinded/damaged Broken Snout with Eye Bite and killed it with a critical spell attack.
- Grond destroyed the final animated lizardfolk statue.
- The party ended with the belt recovered but the mound not fully explored.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- [uncertain] The half-formed skeleton seems connected to the disturbed elven burial, but its exact origin is not fully confirmed.
- [uncertain] The final state of the fire trap after combat is unresolved.
