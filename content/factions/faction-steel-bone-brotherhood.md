# Faction: Steel Bone Brotherhood

## Summary

- Ominous tomb-delving faction tied to masks, undead, red-robed watchers, dangerous Barrow Mounds expeditions, and probable black arts. Session 12 adds a very likely but still unconfirmed link to the serpent-skull marked mound and the Mort / half-orc church-worker lead.

## Current Status

- Active and increasingly likely to be embedded around Helix and the Barrow Mounds. The party has strong circumstantial evidence, but still lacks direct confirmation of the serpent-skull mound's allegiance or Mort's status.

## Goals / Pressure

- Control, exploitation, and surveillance of Barrow Mounds sites, especially around the obelisk-area leads, the prominent barrow cluster, and now very likely the serpent-skull marked mound.

## Known Facts

- The Brotherhood recruits hirelings in [[location-helix|Helix]] for tomb expeditions that locals treat as ominous or near-suicidal.
- Two apparent undead former hirelings in Session 05 were strongly linked in context to earlier Brotherhood activity.
- Red-robed, steel-bone-masked cultists appear to function as low-level field operatives or watchers around the mounds.
- The party associates the Brotherhood with black arts, undead manipulation, and the broader obelisk-area barrow threat.
- In Session 08, [[npc-gant|Gant]] privately warned [[pc-sab|Sab]] that [[npc-mort|Mort]] of the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] may be connected to the Brotherhood and handed over a half-mask found under Mort's bed.
- Wade ruled the Session 12 serpent-around-skull mark is unconfirmed but very likely Brotherhood-related.

## What The Players Know

- The party now treats the [[npc-steel-bone-cultist|Steel Bone cultist]] sightings as the Brotherhood's lowest visible operatives in the field.
- Circumstantial but repeated evidence links the Brotherhood to the mound north of the obelisk, the prominent barrow, red-robed cultist sightings, and Mort / the Fair Church concern.
- Session 12 gave the party a specific mound to surveil: the [[location-serpent-skull-marked-mound|serpent-skull marked mound]].

- The party still lacks direct confirmation of who leads the Brotherhood or how large it is.
- It remains possible that some cultist sightings were magical projections rather than flesh-and-blood operatives.
- Mort's actual relationship to the Brotherhood is still unproven; the half-mask and Session 12 lead suggest danger, but not yet confirmed membership or command.
- The serpent-skull marked mound is very likely Brotherhood-related, but not confirmed by in-world identification yet.
