## Reference

- **[Celestia's merchant interest](faction-celestia-s-merchant-interest)** — Commercial side represented by Celestia, positioned as preferable to Osric.
- **[Fair Church](faction-fair-church)** — Helix church faction pulled into the Mort investigation by Gant's warning, the half-mask beneath Mort's bed, and the missing corpse. Mort's exact allegiance remains unconfirmed.
- **[Iron Chain](faction-iron-chain)** — Rival adventuring party also pursuing the chalice/sledgehammer lead.
- **[Mothers of the Hinge](faction-mothers-of-the-hinge)** — Religious or temple-associated group localized exclusively to the City of Ore and Furnace.
- **[Osric's merchant interest](faction-osric-s-merchant-interest)** — Commercial side represented by Osric, focused on curio trading and hard bargaining.
- **[Temple / Mother-aligned local authority](faction-temple-mother-aligned-local-authority)** — Religious or temple-centered group controlling the settlement reached through the portal.
- **[Thornswild elven tribes](faction-thornswild-elven-tribes)** — Elven people connected to the Thornswild region, the Belt of the Thornswild, and the flooded tomb's wall scenes. Vakish says they lived in a great forest north or east of the mounds before the fog cut off contact.
