# PC: Dern

## Summary

- Dern is a dwarf Sea Wolf / Viking-style raider who entered play during the same death-return sequence that brought Oogie back.
- He was introduced in a liminal devil scene, received an Archie ticket, and then dropped back into Helix to join the party.
- The current sources treat him as a new party companion, but his long-term role is still not fully normalized.

## Current Status

- Alive and active with the party in Helix after Session 07.
- Last seen arriving from the devil-mediated return scene wearing his own gear and carrying a ticket.
- The only explicit task attached to him so far is the devil's instruction to look after Oogie.

## Party Role / Class

- Dwarf Sea Wolf / dwarf raider; the sources also frame him as a Viking-style fighter.
- Appears to be a front-line martial PC rather than a caster or specialist.
- Exact class wording is still noisy, but shield/greataxe/spear/leather-armor gear points to a straightforward armored melee role.

## Inventory / Gear Notes

- Arrived wearing his own gear and carrying an Archie ticket.
- Session 07 source material associates him with a shield, greataxe, spear, and leather armor.
- No other inventory, money, or special equipment is yet clearly grounded in the available sources.
- Unlike Oogie's return, there is not yet a detailed post-death re-equipping scene for Dern.

## Relationships

- Oogie is his clearest relationship anchor: the devil explicitly told Dern to look after him.
- The party adopted Dern immediately after the return scene, but the sources do not yet formalize whether he is a full PC, hireling, or something in between.
- The devil figure is the only other strong tie so far, because it handled Dern's return and instruction.

## Session History

- 2026-03-08 [[2026_0308 Session 07]] — Appeared in a devil-run afterlife limbo alongside Oogie, received an Archie ticket, was sent back to Helix, and joined the party on the spot.

## Notes / Uncertainty

- Dern's backstory is only supported by the shipwreck vignette from Session 07, so it should stay light until later play confirms more.
- The "Sea Wolf" / "Viking-esque" / "raider" labels are consistent in spirit but not yet fully standardized across notes.
- His exact standing in the party is still open: permanent PC, short-term ally, or something else.
