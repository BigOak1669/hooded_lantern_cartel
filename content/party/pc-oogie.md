# PC: Oogie

## Summary

- Fungal seer who used Trance on Gron, giving Gron a luck token and sharing a vision of a nearby chamber with a tablet and ornate urn. Oogie later investigated and read the chaotic tablet, causing it to turn to ash and briefly reveal a map of the Barrow Mounds and a castle beyond them.

## Current Status

- Alive and active with the party. He has the rough tablet-map copy and currently holds the unidentified [[item-cobra-headed-staff|cobra-headed staff]].

## Party Role / Class

- Seer.
- Fungal / mycelian / mushroom-bodied party member; that physiology mattered directly when the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] chose the cemetery compost pile as the best place to regrow or restore him.
- In play he reads as a lightly armored occult support / utility caster rather than a front-line bruiser.
- Session 08 confirms the seer label most cleanly; earlier sources are noisier but consistent with that role.
- Seer; fungal/mushroom person

## Inventory / Gear Notes

- Confirmed ongoing item: half [[item-archie-ticket|Archie ticket]] retained after Oogie's return.
- Session 06: Oogie was carrying standard adventuring gear during the barrow expedition and personally killed one of the plague rats before being isolated by the trap in the deeper mound.
- Session 07 makes his inventory messy: while the party retreated with his body, multiple loose items reportedly spilled or were lost, including torches, food/rations, flint and steel, and staff- or pole-type gear.
- After the resurrection, the party re-equipped him only partially. The clearest repeatedly supported post-return kit is leather armor, a dagger, and caltrops.
- The four amber vials and the gold ring connected to Oogie's recent expedition were sold to [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]] in Session 07, so they are not part of Oogie's current gear.
- Session 08 adds a large iridescent anchor chest tattoo from [[npc-sira-vey|Sira Vey]]. It is presented as body art, not confirmed magical gear.
- rough copied Barrow Mounds map
- returning thrown dagger received from Sab
- spears purchased after returning to Helix

## Relationships

- [[pc-sab|Sab]], [[pc-orlin|Orlin]], [[pc-gradrick|Gradrick]], and [[pc-grond|Grond]] are his established core companions across the related sessions; they carried him out, got him back to Helix, and helped restore and re-equip him.
- [[npc-finn|Finn]] was the hireling present during Oogie's fall and retreat, helped with the emergency extraction, and remained part of the Session 07 aftermath before being paid off.
- [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] became a meaningful support institution in Oogie's continuity because Bartholomew and Gant facilitated his resurrection there despite Mort's hostility.
- [[pc-dern|Dern]] is linked to Oogie very specifically through the death-return scene: the devil told Dern to look after Oogie, which is the clearest basis for their initial relationship.
- [[npc-gant|Gant]] is indirectly important to Oogie as one of the church figures who helped make his return possible.
- Oogie now has a light social tie to [[npc-sira-vey|Sira Vey]] after meeting her during the Helix celebration, using Trance on her, and receiving his tattoo from her.
- The [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]] is not a personal relationship, but Oogie's death, resurrection, and later suspicions all sit close to their barrow activity and imagery.
- Gron: shared Trance vision and map-copying effort
- Arch / Archie: sensed the red devil's laugh when the chaotic tablet chamber was approached
