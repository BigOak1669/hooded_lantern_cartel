# PC: Grond

## Summary

- Front-line party member who wore the gold unicorn-horn belt, benefiting from increased strength. He shared Oogie's Trance vision, helped copy the fading map, fell into a spike pit trap in the tablet chamber, and later chose to keep the Belt of the Thornswild rather than return it to Vakish.

## Current Status

- Alive; healed after spike-pit damage; currently holder/wearer of the Belt of the Thornswild.

## Party Role / Class

- Kobold adventurer.
- Functions in play as a melee frontliner / bruiser rather than a cautious backliner.
- Session 08 explicitly describes him as a pit fighter, which is the clearest class-or-background-style label currently supported in surviving sources.
- Also uses ranged weapons when needed; earlier sessions specifically show him with a bow before the sledgehammer/warhammer-heavy Helix material takes over.
- Martial/front-line adventurer; exact class unclear

## Inventory / Gear Notes

- Strongly associated with [[item-fine-sledgehammer|Werner's fine sledgehammer]] after the Session 05 camp fight; later sessions repeatedly show him using that weapon to break open sealed mounds and in melee.
- Session 05 fallout also suggests he likely ended up with recovered chainmail, though ownership is a little messy in transcript-level material.
- Recovered the [[item-gold-ring|gold ring]] from an already-looted mound in Session 06 and later carried or presented it when the party sold loot at the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] in Session 07.
- Present for the party's handling of the [[item-bag-of-holding|bag of holding]] and the four amber vials, but those items are not cleanly tracked as Grond-held.
- Session 08 combat text calls his melee weapon a warhammer while surrounding continuity points to Werner's sledgehammer; treat that as probable normalization drift rather than clean separate gear unless later notes clarify it.
- In Session 10, Grond found a metal cylindrical tube in a flooded sarcophagus; the note does not establish him as its long-term owner.
- In Session 11, Grond wore the [[item-hero-belt-gold-and-unicorn-horn-buckle|hero belt / gold-and-unicorn-horn belt buckle]] at session end. Its observed property is +4 Strength while worn; any other properties remain unknown.
- Belt of the Thornswild / gold unicorn-horn belt
- sledgehammer referenced from prior sessions

## Relationships

- Core party companion to [[pc-sab|Sab]], [[pc-oogie|Oogie]], [[pc-orlin|Orlin]], and [[pc-gradrick|Gradrick]], with later continuity also placing him alongside [[pc-dern|Dern]].
- Has a notably practical combat partnership with Sab: Grond pushes into danger or forces openings, while Sab repeatedly covers the party with holy power, crossbow fire, and emergency support.
- Oogie explicitly tags Grond as the party's “ragey type melee character” when choosing support magic in Session 08, which is one of the clearest intra-party role reads in the surviving transcript.
- Built unusually positive Helix ties for a bruiser PC: [[npc-bolo|Bolo]] publicly thanked him during the feast, [[npc-billsworth|Billsworth]] welcomed his help unloading cargo, and local children warmed to him after his storytime scene.
- Had a pragmatic but strained hireling relationship with [[npc-finn|Finn]]: Finn repeatedly served as the party's lantern-bearer, while Grond also voiced frustration when Finn panicked under pressure.
- His arc is tightly entangled with [[npc-werner|Werner]]'s lost hammer: Grond helped chase the lead, refused to disarm during Nabu's hostage play, and likely ended up carrying Werner's weapon after Werner's death.
- Oogie: shared vision and map-copying effort
- Vakish Baharus: kept Vakish's requested belt as an agreed exchange/reward
