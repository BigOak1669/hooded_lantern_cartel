# PC: Gradrick

## Summary

- Spellcaster/witch-type PC who accepted the Charm Person scroll assignment during the opening inventory cleanup, helped read faded writing in the burial chamber, and helped manage light as the party moved through the flooded tomb.

## Current Status

- Alive and active with the party. Assigned holder of the Charm Person scroll.

## Party Role / Class

- Witch.
- Reads as back-rank magical support / striker rather than a frontliner.
- Confirmed spell use in the linked sessions includes **Eye Bite**, used both on the road to Helix and in the northern barrow, and again during the Session 07 retreat scene against the red-robed watcher/illusion.
- By the Session 07 post-carouse bookkeeping, Sam/Gradrick was part of the group hitting level 2, though the note does not preserve a full updated build summary.
- Witch / spellcaster

## Inventory / Gear Notes

- No personal signature weapon or custom gear is cleanly established in the linked notes.
- Gradrick wears witch's robes in the Session 07 transcript description.
- He participated in party treasure flow around the Helix arc rather than being singled out as the holder of specific loot: the group sold the shrine gold coin earlier, pursued Celestia's chalice job, identified the invisibility potion, sold the gold ring and amber vials to Vakish, and pooled 100 gold for the Brazen Strumpet carouse.
- No Gradrick-specific carried item is solid enough from these sources to treat as canon beyond ordinary spellcasting gear. The [[item-charm-person-spell-scroll|Charm Person spell scroll]] is a witch spell and was identified by Gradrick, but its final holder is not clearly stated.
- Charm Person scroll

## Relationships

- **Sab** — regular party partner in fights; Sab often opens with divine or ranged pressure while Gradrick follows with Eye Bite or other witch support.
- **Grond** — frequent combat counterpart; Grond handles melee or ranged physical pressure while Gradrick adds magical follow-through.
- **Orlin** — consistent fellow explorer from the early dungeon/ticket arc through the Helix run.
- **Oogie** — part of the same core Helix/barrow party; Gradrick is present through Oogie's death, return route, and resurrection aftermath.
- **Dern** — by Session 07, Dern is folded into the party during Oogie's return sequence, putting Gradrick in the first recorded party lineup that includes him.
- **Celestia / Werner / Maza / Vakish / Fair Church contacts** — these are party-level relationships tied to the chalice and Helix support network; Gradrick is present in that continuity, but the sources do not show him as the main speaker with any one of them.
- Orlin: collaborated on reading the chamber writing
