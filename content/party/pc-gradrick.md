# Gradrick

## At a Glance

Gradrick is the party's witch, a back-line spellcaster whose Eye Bite and practical magical knowledge have supported the group since its earliest recorded delves.

## Current Situation

Gradrick is active in Helix. His crystal owl is watching the serpent-and-skull marked mound, where it exposed Mouse's delivery routine. Mazzah recently gave him a small spellbook containing Hypnotize.

## Defining History

- Helped finish the corpse-eating creature on the road to Helix and damaged the northern barrow's gray ooze with Eye Bite.
- Supported the retreat from the red-robed watcher after Oogie's fall.
- Helped decipher the faded Thornswild writing in the flooded ceremonial tomb.
- Accepted custody of the Charm Person scroll recovered from a flooded sarcophagus.
- Used the [[item-crystal-owl|Owl of Magnus]] to watch the marked mound and witnessed Mouse's knock signal and delivery to its large occupant.

## Notable Possessions

- [[item-crystal-owl|Owl of Magnus]], currently deployed for surveillance
- Charm Person spell scroll
- Hypnotize spellbook

## Relationships

- **Mazzah:** magical consultant and current custodian of the party's dangerous skin-bound spellbook.
- **Mouse:** observed through the owl while Mouse supplied the marked mound.
- **The party:** long-standing magical support across the dungeon, Helix, and Barrow Mounds arcs.

## Uncertainty

Transcript spellings of Gradrick's name are unusually unstable; the aliases above refer to the same PC.
