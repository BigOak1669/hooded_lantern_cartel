# PC: Sab

## Summary

- Priestly halfling/Old God devotee who inspected doors, performed a funerary prayer over the Thornswild burial, recovered the magically preserved shroud/cloak, healed Gron after the spike pit, and later returned Vakish's borrowed cloak so the party could keep the belt. Mazah identified Sab's recovered shroud as a Cloak of Elvenkind.

## Current Status

- Alive; currently associated with the Cloak of Elvenkind unless the table later assigns it elsewhere.

## Party Role / Class

- Priest / healer-oriented divine caster.
- Clearly has access to **Turn Undead**, which was decisive in both Session 05 and Session 06.
- Also uses halfling invisibility in play, which made Sab the party's best documented scout in Session 05.
- In practice, Sab reads as a flexible support character: anti-undead specialist, backup ranged attacker, and cautious problem-solver rather than a pure frontliner.
- The exact religious wording in sources is a little noisy, but Sab is treated as a worshiper of an old god tradition and was legible as such to the Fair Church.
- Priest / cleric-type; worships Odin/Allfather or Old Gods; halfling invisibility mentioned

## Inventory / Gear Notes

- Carries the [[item-returning-dagger|returning dagger]] given by [[npc-tiramel|Tiramelle]] after the northern barrow rescue.
- Confirmed property: when thrown, it returns to Sab's hand.
- Fuller magical properties remain unidentified because Sab declined to leave it with [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]] for study.
- Frequently fights with a crossbow in the surviving sources.
- Opened the road fight in Session 03 with a surprise shot.
- Killed the gray ooze with a blessed crossbow bolt in Session 04.
- Killed a fleeing raider with a crossbow in Session 08.
- Had a longsword taken temporarily by Ikram during the Session 05 Werner/Nabu confrontation, then recovered afterward in the surviving transcript.
- Put the four ornate amber vials into the bag of holding in Session 06 before the party later sold them in Session 07.
- Received two nonmagical raven shoulder tattoos in Session 08.
- Took custody of a Steel Bone Brotherhood half-mask found under Mort's bed in Session 08.
- In Session 11, appears to have the [[item-two-silver-diamond-rings|two small silver diamond rings]] at session end unless later play returns or relocates them.
- Cloak of Elvenkind
- returning thrown dagger transferred to Oogie

## Relationships

- Core party companion to Oogie, Orlin, Gradrick, and Grond across the Helix barrow arc.
- Worked especially closely beside Orlin in Session 04, where the two listened at the barred chamber door together inside the northern barrow.
- Earned or at least kept Tiramelle's trust strongly enough to retain Tiramelle's returning dagger after the northern barrow events.
- Helped manage Oogie's aftermath after the Session 06 disaster: carried him out with the party, took part in the Fair Church approach, and stayed involved through the resurrection/recovery sequence in Session 07.
- Has a useful tie to the Fair Church through Gant, who trusted Sab privately with his suspicions about Mort and handed her the Brotherhood half-mask.
- Mort is now a live suspicion point for Sab specifically, but the source support does **not** yet prove Mort is actually Brotherhood-aligned.
- Gron: healed him after spike-pit injury
- Mazah: brought cloak for identification
- Oogie: gave him the returning thrown dagger
