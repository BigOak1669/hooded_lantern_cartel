# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party converted the goblet lead into its first organized Barrow Mounds expedition. They hired Finn, met Mazzah, identified the invisibility potion, and entered a freshly disturbed northern barrow. After killing a gray ooze, they freed Ikram and Tiramel and learned that Nabo had betrayed the Iron Chain and escaped with Werner's sledgehammer.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

In Helix, the party watched the Steel Bone Brotherhood recruit hirelings and hired Finn after he produced a fuller map of the mounds. Mazzah demonstrated that Werner's silver goblet could create mead once per day and identified the roadside vial as a potion of invisibility. At the mounds, Finn helped the party compare the torn-map site, an older southern mound, and a fresher northern barrow. They chose the northern site, found traps, a mummy, a fresh corpse, and a gray ooze, then opened a barred room containing Ikram and Tiramel. The survivors said their companion Nabo had betrayed them and fled with Werner's hammer; Perry had been left dead.

## Key Scenes / Beats

- The Steel Bone Brotherhood recruited openly in Helix.
- Finn's map reframed the barrows as a larger field of sites.
- Mazzah identified the potion and demonstrated the goblet.
- The northern barrow exposed Nabo's betrayal and the Iron Chain's collapse.
