# Session Note

## Executive Summary

The session focused on turning the [[location-helix|Helix]] chalice lead into an actionable barrow expedition. After reviewing what they knew about [[npc-werner|Werner]], [[npc-mazzah|Maza]], and the rival [[faction-iron-chain|Iron Chain]] party, the group hired a torchbearer named [[npc-finn|Finn]], visited Maza’s tower to inspect the chalice and identify one of their unknown items, then went out to the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]]. There they confirmed which nearby mounds were plausible targets, entered a freshly disturbed barrow, briefly fought a corrosive ooze, freed two betrayed members of the Iron Chain, learned that the goblin [[npc-nabo|Nabo]] likely escaped with Werner’s lucky sledgehammer, and ended with a clear new pursuit back toward Helix.

## What Happened

The session opened with an in-character recap of the road to [[location-helix|Helix]] and the situation at the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]]: the party had come on [[npc-celestia|Celestia]]’s behalf to recover a [[item-silver-chalice|silver chalice]] tied to [[npc-werner|Werner]], a drunk in Helix, but learned the chalice was temporarily in the hands of the wizard [[npc-mazzah|Maza]] for study. They also reviewed the road encounter with the ghoul, the scrap of barrow map, the letter for [[npc-tacey|Tacey]], the hound that now follows them, and the unidentified vial recovered from the ghoul.

The party debated whether to confront [[npc-mazzah|Maza]], chase the rival [[faction-iron-chain|Iron Chain]] party, or head straight to the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]] to recover [[npc-werner|Werner]]’s lost sledgehammer. Their discussion made it clear they preferred negotiation and information gathering over starting a fight with either a wizard or another adventuring party.

The next morning in [[location-helix|Helix]], the party encountered the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]] recruiting hirelings for a tomb expedition. The Brotherhood’s presence reinforced the danger of the mounds. The party then questioned local hirelings about which barrow [[npc-werner|Werner]] had looted. One nervous local, [[npc-rusk|Rusk]], tried to monetize the information, but a second prospect — a lantern-bearing torchbearer named [[npc-finn|Finn]] — proved more useful. Finn showed them a fuller map of the mounds, identified the [[location-sealed-mound-on-torn-map|sealed mound]] on their scrap, and narrowed the likely source of the chalice to one of the two nearby mounds. The party hired Finn.

Before heading out, the group went to [[npc-mazzah|Maza]]’s tower. Gronn loudly knocked despite the posted warning, and the party’s joking “show us your chalice” chant brought Maza to the door. Maza let them inside. He showed them the [[item-silver-chalice|silver chalice]] and demonstrated that it can fill once per day with liquid; at least one confirmed output was mead. He appeared cagey about whether it can produce other liquids, but strongly implied it can. During the visit, Maza also identified the seemingly empty vial from the ghoul as a [[item-potion-of-invisibility|potion of invisibility]]. He invited the party to bring him other odd magical items in the future.

[[npc-finn|Finn]] then guided the party to the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]]. They examined the [[location-sealed-mound-on-torn-map|sealed mound]] on their map and confirmed it would require breaking in. They checked a southern mound whose broken entrance appeared old and not recently used, then moved to a northern mound where fresher disturbance and tracks suggested recent activity, likely the [[faction-iron-chain|Iron Chain]].

Inside the [[location-northern-barrow|northern barrow]], the party found a central chamber with a barred western door, a more open eastern chamber containing a standing sarcophagus with a mummy and a fresh corpse, and signs that someone had already passed through. Sab and Orlin listened at the barred door and heard a man and woman arguing quietly on the far side.

In the eastern chamber, the fresh corpse proved dangerous by proximity to a gray ooze. When the ooze animated and attacked, the party fought a short combat. Gronn’s arrows showed the creature’s corrosive effect, Gradrick damaged it with Eye Bite, and Sab killed it with a blessed crossbow bolt.

At the same time, the party opened the barred room and freed two members of the [[faction-iron-chain|Iron Chain]]: [[npc-ikram|Ikram]] and Tiramel the Red. These two explained they had entered seeking [[npc-werner|Werner]]’s sledgehammer and had been betrayed. They believed their goblin companion [[npc-nabo|Nabo]] locked them in, killed or abandoned their halfling ally [[npc-perry|Perry]], and escaped with the sledgehammer. Tiramel also handed over a fine-looking dagger found in the barrow as thanks for being freed.

By the end of the session, the immediate barrow fight was over, the likely betrayal inside the [[faction-iron-chain|Iron Chain]] was clear, and the party’s direction shifted from deeper exploration toward pursuing [[npc-nabo|Nabo]] and the hammer back toward [[location-helix|Helix]].
