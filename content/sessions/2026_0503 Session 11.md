# Session Note

## Executive Summary

The party continued [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]]' belt-buckle lead by entering the third of his three marked mounds in the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]]. They identified the scroll from the earlier metal tube as a [[item-charm-person-spell-scroll|Charm Person spell scroll]], explored a flooded ceremonial tomb, managed a hidden oil-fire trap, disturbed elven funerary remains, and found a sculptural battle chamber containing the likely belt/buckle Vakish wanted. [[pc-grond|Grond]] removed and wore the [[item-hero-belt-gold-and-unicorn-horn-buckle|hero belt]], which magically fit him and granted +4 Strength while worn. Removing it extinguished a pale sword light and animated three stone lizardfolk. The party destroyed the statues and a half-formed skeleton threat, ending alive with the belt in hand but the mound not fully explored.

## What Happened

- The group recapped that they had resupplied in [[location-helix|Helix]] and were following [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]]' lead from the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] toward three mounds.
- [[pc-grond|Grond]] opened the metal tube recovered in Session 10, and [[pc-gradrick|Gradrick]] identified the parchment inside as the witch spell [[item-charm-person-spell-scroll|Charm Person]].
- The party entered the [[location-third-vakish-mound|third Vakish mound]] through a stone seal already cracked open by Grond, passed a damp iron-banded wooden door, and descended into a shallow flooded ceremonial chamber.
- [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] carried torchlight while [[pc-gradrick|Gradrick]] managed lantern or torch light. [[npc-finn|Finn]] was not present and was not available for this delve.
- [[pc-sab|Sab]] found an untrapped swiveling stone door into a hidden funerary alcove with a half-submerged stone altar and ornate box.
- Oogie detected another hidden door protected by rancid oil, overhead pipes or barrels, and an underwater mechanism. He triggered the fire trap from a distance, and Grond wedged the mechanism with a rock.
- [[pc-orlin|Orlin]] mishandled the funerary box, spilling elven ash, bones, and trinkets into the water. Sab restored the bones, prayed over them, and recovered [[item-two-silver-diamond-rings|two small silver rings set with diamonds]].
- The party discovered a larger chamber showing a wounded elven warrior statue with a pale glowing sword facing three lizardfolk statues. A ceremonial belt with a worked buckle was around the elf's waist.
- Grond confirmed that the belt matched Vakish's description: solid gold with pale unicorn-horn or ivory-like inlay. When he removed it, the sword light went dark and the lizardfolk statues animated.
- Grond put on the belt; it magically fit him and granted +4 Strength while worn. No additional belt properties, risks, or curses are confirmed.
- In combat, one lizardfolk statue called Razor Fang hit Grond for 1 damage. Sab destroyed another statue with a critical crossbow shot.
- Oogie accidentally dislodged the rock that had held the fire trap, reactivating continuous flame. Orlin crossed the fire, took 1 fire damage, transformed into a bear, and later received Rage from Oogie.
- Gradrick used Eye Bite against one statue, later called Broken Snout, and ultimately killed it with a critical spell attack.
- A half-formed skeleton, apparently connected to the disturbed funerary remains, attacked Orlin through or from the fire and missed. Orlin destroyed it while in enraged bear form.
- Grond smashed the final animated lizardfolk statue apart. The session ended immediately after combat with the belt recovered and the mound still unfinished.
