# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party entered the third Vakish mound, a flooded ceremonial tomb preserving scenes of an elven champion and lizardfolk enemies. They identified the recovered scroll as Charm Person, navigated an oil-fire trap, disturbed and partly restored elven remains, and claimed a gold-and-unicorn-horn hero belt. Removing the belt animated three lizardfolk statues and a half-formed skeleton, which the party destroyed.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

Gradrick identified the metal tube's scroll as Charm Person. Inside the mound, shallow black water concealed mechanisms and hidden doors. The party found a funerary alcove with elven remains and two silver diamond rings; Sab restored the disturbed burial as best she could. Oogie and Grond worked out the oil-fire trap. In a larger tableau, an elven warrior statue held a pale glowing sword and wore the belt Vakish had described while three stone lizardfolk threatened it. Grond removed and wore the belt, which magically fit and granted +4 Strength. The sword went dark and the statues animated. The party defeated them and a half-formed skeleton, but ended before completing the mound.
