## Executive Summary

The party pursued the first part of Vakish's belt lead, clearing a hidden crypt threatened by purple fungal masses. They recovered gems, two heavy gold jackals, a crystal owl, and an Alarm scroll, then returned to Helix when carrying capacity became the limiting danger. Mazzah identified the owl as the Owl of Magnus, and Vakish bought one jackal and the loose gems.

## What Happened

After checking in at the Rose Quartz, the party returned to the Barrow Mounds and entered the first northern candidate from Vakish's directions. A hidden inner crypt contained hostile purple fungal masses, gem-studded funerary statues, and a trapped altar room. The party defeated the fungi, extracted rubies and black opals, and recovered two gold jackal statues, a crystal owl, and a scroll tube. They withdrew rather than overextend while heavily loaded. In Helix, Mazzah identified the crystal owl as the Owl of Magnus and the parchment as an Alarm spell scroll. The party left the owl and scroll with him, sold one jackal and the gems to Vakish, and retained the second jackal for later recovery or sale.
