# Session Note

## Executive Summary

The party began the day in [[location-helix|Helix]] after the previous session’s carousing, checked in with the Rose Quartz, and headed back into the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]] to pursue [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish]]’s belt-buckle lead. They found a hidden inner crypt, fought [[encounter-hostile-purple-fungal-masses|hostile purple fungal masses]], harvested gems from funerary statues, uncovered a trapped altar room containing a [[item-crystal-owl|crystal owl]] and two heavy [[item-gold-jackal-statue|gold jackal statue]]s, and decided to retreat once bag space became the limiting factor. Back in Helix, Mazah identified the crystal owl as the Owl of Magnus and the parchment in the metal tube as an [[item-alarm-spell-scroll|Alarm spell scroll]]. The party banked the owl and scroll with Mazah, sold one gold jackal plus the loose gems to Vakish, and ended the session safe in town with Mort investigation and a second trip to the mound both strongly in play.

## What Happened

- The party recapped the previous night’s revelry in [[location-helix|Helix]], including a raider attack at the Rose Quartz.
- They reviewed [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish]]’s belt-buckle lead and the map of the candidate mounds.
- The party traveled to the first northern candidate mound, broke through the exterior stone covering, and found a brass inner door and an entry crypt lined with large female statues whose eyes were set with gems.
- A hidden pivoting false wall led to a larger, foul-smelling inner chamber with a central debris heap that was the nest of hostile purple mushroom-creatures.
- The party used direct force and fire to destroy the living fungus, then harvested gems from the funerary statues and triggered a gas trap in the severe rear statue.
- A second hidden pivot-door led to a cleaner altar room with two [[item-gold-jackal-statue|gold jackal statue]]s, a [[item-crystal-owl|crystal owl]], and a hinged pit trap with a mechanical snake below.
- The party retrieved the owl and one jackal, then retreated due to bag-space limits.
- They opened the metal tube outside and found a spell parchment, which Mazah identified as an [[item-alarm-spell-scroll|Alarm spell scroll]].
- The party banked the owl and scroll with Mazah and sold one gold jackal plus the loose gems to [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish]] for 120 gold.
