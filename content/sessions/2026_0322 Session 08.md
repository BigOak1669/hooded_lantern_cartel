# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party's Brazen Strumpet carouse became a town-wide feast built around Billsworth's Blackbark gin and starshine eel. Amid new friendships and tattoos, Gant privately warned Sab that Mort might be tied to the Steel Bone Brotherhood. The celebration ended in a predawn raid on Billsworth's cart and the Rose Quartz, which the intoxicated party and Vakish's mechanical guards survived.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

The party helped Billsworth and Bolo unload fine gin and a starshine eel, then shared the resulting feast with Helix. Grond entertained local children and earned public goodwill. The traveling tattoo artist Sira Vey worked during the celebration; Oogie used Trance on her and received an iridescent anchor tattoo, while Sab received raven tattoos. Away from the crowd, Gant gave Sab a half-mask found beneath Mort's bed and voiced suspicion about the half-orc church worker. Before dawn, attackers struck Billsworth's cart and the Rose Quartz at the same time. The party fought while heavily drunk; Vakish's mechanical guards killed several raiders, and the survivors finally returned to the Brazen Strumpet to rest.

## Key Scenes / Beats

- The carouse strengthened the party's place in Helix.
- Sira Vey became a colorful traveling contact.
- Gant handed Sab the mask connected to Mort.
- The coordinated raid targeted two valuable merchant sites.
