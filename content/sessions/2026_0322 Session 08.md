# Session Note

## Executive Summary

Session 08 played out the party's successful carousing result at [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]]. Fresh off their sale to [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] proprietor [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]], the party helped [[npc-billsworth|Billsworth]] and [[npc-bolo|Bolo]] unload fine [[item-blackbark-gin|Blackbark gin]] and a [[item-starshine-eel|starshine eel]], turned the night into a town-wide feast, met the traveling tattoo artist [[npc-sira-vey|Sira Vey]], and deepened local ties in [[location-helix|Helix]]. The most important continuity development came when [[npc-gant|Gant]] quietly warned [[pc-sab|Sab]] that [[npc-mort|Mort]] of the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] may be tied to the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]] and handed her a half-mask found under Mort's bed. The celebration then flipped into danger when raiders hit both Billsworth's cart and the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] at once; Vakish's robot guards killed several attackers, the party fought while badly drunk, and the group finally took rooms at the Brazen Strumpet, rested, and handled level-up discussion.

## What Happened

- The party, leaving the Rose Quartz with fresh coin after selling recent loot, noticed Billsworth and Bolo unloading casks and an iced starshine eel outside the Brazen Strumpet.
- The party spent its pooled carousing money on a public feast, with Bolo contributing toward turning the gin and eel into a town-facing celebration.
- [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] met [[npc-sira-vey|Sira Vey]], used Trance on her, and later received a large iridescent anchor chest tattoo.
- [[pc-sab|Sab]] discovered the starshine eel, later received two raven shoulder tattoos, and was privately trusted with Gant's warning about Mort and the hidden Steel Bone Brotherhood half-mask.
- [[pc-grond|Grond]] helped unload the wagon, kept the social energy moving, entertained local children, lost an arm-wrestling bet, gave a public speech of thanks, and fought during the raid.
- In the early morning, raiders struck both Billsworth's cart and the Rose Quartz; Vakish's robot guards and the party killed several attackers, and the party ended the night by taking rooms at the Brazen Strumpet.

- Billsworth brought fine casks of Blackbark gin and a rare starshine eel into Helix.

- Vakish said to come back in the morning because he might have a reward for the party.

## Encounters / Combat Outcomes

The main combat encounter was the predawn raid outside the Brazen Strumpet and Rose Quartz. While badly drunk, the party ran into an attack already in progress. Vakish's robot guards killed one raider by ripping them in half and smashed or tackled others. Grond badly wounded one attacker in the leg. Sab killed a fleeing raider with a crossbow shot. Oogie killed a crawling wounded raider at close range. At least one attacker appears to have escaped. One of Vakish's own robots also seized Sab, called her an intruder, and nearly tore her apart before Vakish ordered it to stand down.
