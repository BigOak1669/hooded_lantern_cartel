# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party began in [[location-helix|Helix]], fully rested after the prior session’s fallout over the [[item-spent-chalice|spent chalice]], and returned to the Barrow Mounds with [[npc-finn|Finn]] as lantern-bearer. They cleared two mounds: one already-raided tomb yielded only a [[item-gold-ring|gold ring]], while a sealed mound released undead plague rats and then rewarded the party with four small amber vials from a nested casket. Throughout the expedition, a [[npc-steel-bone-cultist|red-robed figure]] believed to be tied to the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]] repeatedly watched the party from the mist and seemed to guide or lure them toward a more prominent mound. The session ended badly in that mound’s lower chamber: [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] triggered a trap, got isolated with marked skeletons, the party learned those skeletons explode on death, and Oogie was carried back toward Helix in critical condition.

## What Happened

The group opened with a recap of recent events in and around [[location-helix|Helix]]: Werner and Nabo were killed in the prior session, the chalice was recovered but appears spent, [[npc-mazzah|Mazod]] identified the [[item-bag-of-holding|bag of holding]] as having five slots, and a note pointed toward a tomb north of the obelisk and referenced the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]].

Back in [[location-helix|Helix]], the party debated whether to engage the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]] directly or continue barrow exploration. They rehired [[npc-finn|Finn]] as lantern-bearer and went back out into the Barrow Mounds.

The first mound they checked this session was already open and apparently looted. Inside was only a disturbed skeleton and a ring, which Grand recovered. When the party came back out, [[pc-orlin|Orlin]] and [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] spotted a distant [[npc-steel-bone-cultist|red-robed figure]] at the edge of the mist. The figure did not approach and disappeared when observed closely.

The party then opened a sealed mound with Grand’s newly acquired sledgehammer. Undead-looking plague rats burst out. [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] killed one, confirming its blood was thick black fluid, and [[pc-sab|Sab]] used Turn Undead to destroy the rest. Inside that mound was a simple sarcophagus containing a second wooden casket shaped like a woman. Inside were skeletal remains and four identical small ornate amber vials. The party took the vials and put them in the [[item-bag-of-holding|bag of holding]].

After leaving that mound, the party again saw the [[npc-steel-bone-cultist|red-robed figure]]. [[pc-orlin|Orlin]] waved; the figure raised a hand in reply, then moved off through the mist in the general direction of the [[location-obelisk-area|obelisk area]]. Rather than open another sealed mound late in the day, the party chose to approach the figure’s apparent destination: a more prominent barrow marked by standing stones and a tripod over a shaft.

Inside that mound, the figure was gone. The party found a block-and-tackle over a hole and climbed roughly 35–40 feet down into an underground chamber with worn frescoes, graffiti, bones, blood smears, and signs of regular traffic. [[pc-sab|Sab]] heard possible chanting from one direction and more animalistic rustling from another; Grand smelled incense.

While scouting, [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] triggered a pressure-plate trap that dropped a stone barrier and temporarily cut him off from the rest of the party. Two skeletons marked with inverted black triangles on their foreheads emerged from the darkness and attacked him. The others managed to reopen the barrier, but not before Oogie was brought down. The party then learned that these skeletons explode into bone shrapnel when destroyed. After killing them, the group stabilized Oogie enough to keep him alive but unconscious, retreated from the dungeon, and headed back toward [[location-helix|Helix]] looking for help. The session ended before treatment was resolved.
