# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party returned to the Barrow Mounds with Finn, cleared two smaller sites, and repeatedly saw a red-robed watcher in the fog. Following that figure toward a prominent barrow led to disaster: Oogie triggered a trap, became isolated with exploding marked skeletons, and was carried back toward Helix in critical condition.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

After regrouping in Helix, the party rehired Finn. An already-open barrow yielded a gold ring. A sealed barrow held plague rats and four ornate amber vials inside a nested casket. During both searches, a red-robed figure watched from the mist and responded when Orlin waved. The figure moved toward a prominent mound with standing stones and a shaft. Below it, the party found signs of repeated traffic, blood, incense, and possible chanting. Oogie triggered a trap that isolated him with skeletons marked by inverted black triangles. The party learned that the skeletons exploded on death and retreated with Oogie down.

## Key Scenes / Beats

- The red-robed watcher appeared more than once and acknowledged Orlin.
- Sab's Turn Undead broke the plague-rat threat.
- The prominent barrow showed signs of active ritual use.
- Oogie's isolation and the exploding skeletons forced a desperate retreat.
