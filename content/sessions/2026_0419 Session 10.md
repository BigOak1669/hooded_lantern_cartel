# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party, having recovered Orlin's healing spell and stabilized Oogie between expeditions, started in Helix and checked two storefronts near the Fair Church: the Silver Strand Merchant Company and a strange familiar door that opened onto a rock gnome shop tied to the Hooded Lantern cartel. After resupplying, they returned to the Barrow Mounds, recovered the remaining gold jackal from a previously cleared mound, entered a water-filled tomb where they battled water zombies, broke through one rusty portcullis, searched flooded sarcophagi, recovered the strange metal tube later identified as [[item-charm-person-spell-scroll|Charm Person]], and left after deciding not to search the sludgy second side room. They then reached the [[location-third-vakish-mound|third Vakish mound]], opened the exterior stone cover and inner door, and stopped there for the session.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

- The party discussed outstanding quests: clearing mounds for Vakish Baharus and the Fair Church / Mort thread. They started in Helix near the Brazen Strumpet and Rose Quartz.
- The party noticed a familiar, unusual door near the Fair Church. Sab bought oil at the Silver Strand Merchant Company, whose human shopkeeper did not recognize or acknowledge the strange door.
- Sab then opened the strange door and found a rock gnome shopkeeper in a sparse shop tied to the Hooded Lantern cartel. The rock gnome recognized that Oogie and Orlin had torn Archie tickets and warned them to be careful.
- The party returned to a previously explored barrow mound, avoided the known trap, recovered the remaining gold jackal, and exited the mound.
- The group moved on to a different water-filled tomb with a sealed covering. Grond smashed the entrance open.
- Inside, water zombies attacked. The party fought them, using Orlin's bear form, a 1d4 spell from Gradrick, and attacks from the rest of the group. The zombies were destroyed.
- The chamber was flooded; a west-side room beyond a rusty portcullis held partially open sarcophagi in the water. Orlin smashed through the portcullis in bear form, Sab helped widen the opening, and the party searched the sarcophagi.
- Grond found a metal cylindrical tube in one sarcophagus. It appeared to be a container with an opening or twist-off end.
- The east-side portcullis led toward the destroyed zombies and putrid water. Sab vocally wanted to leave, and the party opted not to search that sludgy side room.
- The party left the water-filled tomb, continued south past another occupied mound, and reached the [[location-third-vakish-mound|third Vakish mound]]. They broke through the stone cover, exposed a rotten iron-reinforced wooden door, opened it, and ended the session before exploring inside.
