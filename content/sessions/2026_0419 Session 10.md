## Executive Summary

The party resupplied in Helix, discovered a strange Hooded Lantern shop door, and returned to Vakish's mound circuit. They recovered the second gold jackal, fought water zombies in a separate flooded tomb, found a metal scroll tube, and ended after opening the third Vakish mound.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

Near the Fair Church, the party visited the Silver Strand Merchant Company and a familiar-looking door that opened onto a rock-gnome shop tied to the Hooded Lantern cartel. After resupplying, they recovered the gold jackal left behind during the prior expedition. A different water-filled tomb held flooded sarcophagi, rusty portcullises, and water zombies. The party destroyed the undead, searched the accessible chambers, and recovered a metal cylindrical tube, but declined to probe the dirtiest side room. They then reached the third mound from Vakish's lead. Grond broke the stone cover and the party opened the swollen inner door before ending the session.
