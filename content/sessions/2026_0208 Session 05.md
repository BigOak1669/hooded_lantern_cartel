# Session Note

## Executive Summary

The session centered on fallout from the prior tomb expedition: the party tried to track down the goblin who escaped with [[npc-werner|Werner]]’s sledgehammer, discovered evidence tying recent events to the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]], fought two apparent undead former hirelings near the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]], then tracked Werner and [[npc-nabo|Nabu]] to a [[location-camp-outside-helix|camp outside Helix]]. There, Nabu killed [[npc-ikram|Ikram]] after a failed hostage demand, Werner woke and proved violently unstable, and the party killed Werner. In the aftermath they recovered Werner’s [[item-fine-sledgehammer|fine sledgehammer]], a note directing the [[item-chalice|chalice]] to “the [[location-mound-north-of-the-obelisk|mound north of the obelisk]],” and a magical storage bag later identified by [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]] as a bag of holding. By session end, [[npc-celestia|Celestia]] had followed up on her chalice deal, Mazzah confirmed the chalice had run out of power, and the party’s clearest next lead was the marked mound north of the obelisk.

## What Happened

The party began by reviewing the prior outing: they had dealt with [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]], hired [[npc-finn|Finn]] the lantern-bearer, explored multiple barrows, met the trapped pair [[npc-ikram|Ikram]] and [[npc-tiramel|Tiramelle]], and defeated the stone ooze in the tomb. During that recap, the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]] was reaffirmed as an ominous faction in dark velvet robes and steel skull masks, rumored to recruit hirelings who do not return.

Leaving the tomb at dusk, the party tried to determine where the escaped goblin had gone. They could not pick out the goblin’s tracks, but they were approached by two men previously seen among the hirelings who had gone with the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]]. Both appeared undead, with lifeless white eyes. The party fought them; [[pc-sab|Sab]]’s turn undead destroyed one and drove off the other.

The group returned to [[location-helix|Helix]] and checked the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]]. [[npc-werner|Werner]] and the goblin were not there, but [[npc-bolo|Bolo]]/[[npc-tacey|Tacey]] confirmed Werner had been in earlier, had run up a tab of about 66 gold, and had come in with a “little fella.” Outside, [[npc-tiramel|Tiramelle]] was left with the rescued hound while the party and [[npc-ikram|Ikram]] went to check first on [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]]’s tower, then on Tiramelle and Ikram’s camp.

At the camp, [[pc-sab|Sab]] used halfling invisibility to scout ahead and saw [[npc-werner|Werner]] asleep with the sledgehammer. Before the party could act cleanly, [[npc-nabo|Nabu]] emerged from the shadows with a dagger to [[npc-ikram|Ikram]]’s throat and demanded the party disarm. Grond refused to drop weapons, and Nabu immediately slit Ikram’s throat. Sab then stabbed Nabu from invisibility, but when Sab used Nabu as a hostage, Werner responded by crushing Nabu’s head with the sledgehammer and then fighting the party directly. The party eventually brought Werner down.

Looting the bodies revealed [[npc-werner|Werner]]’s [[item-fine-sledgehammer|fine sledgehammer]] and a note reading: “bring the [[item-chalice|chalice]] to the [[location-mound-north-of-the-obelisk|mound north of the obelisk]].” [[npc-nabo|Nabu]]’s remains held a strange black pouch, later identified by [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]] as a bag of holding that seemed to hold about five items. The party also took gear from the dead, including chainmail, and returned to town to rest.

The next morning, [[npc-bolo|Bolo]] delivered a black letter from [[npc-celestia|Celestia]] asking the party to meet her at [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]]’s tower regarding her “investment” in the [[item-chalice|chalice]]. At the tower, Celestia and Mazzah were together. Mazzah confirmed the chalice had run out of charges and no longer filled itself, though he had made healing salves from it before it depleted. The note’s destination was interpreted as likely connected to the [[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]], and Mazzah marked the relevant [[location-mound-north-of-the-obelisk|mound north of the obelisk]] on the party’s map. Mazzah also identified the strange pouch as a bag of holding. The session ended with the party considering the marked mound as the next lead.
