# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The pursuit of Nabo and Werner ended in a deadly confrontation outside Helix. Nabo killed Ikram during a hostage demand, Werner killed Nabo, and the party killed Werner after he attacked them. The recovered hammer, bag of holding, and note to the mound north of the obelisk closed the immediate chase but opened a deeper Steel Bone Brotherhood mystery.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

Leaving the northern barrow, the party failed to track Nabo and fought two apparent undead former hirelings. In Helix, Bolo confirmed that Werner and a goblin had recently visited and that Werner owed a large tavern debt. Sab then scouted Ikram and Tiramel's camp invisibly and found Werner asleep with the sledgehammer. Nabo ambushed Ikram and demanded the party disarm; when they refused, he killed his hostage. Werner woke, killed Nabo, and attacked the party, who killed him. The party recovered the hammer, a black pouch, and a note directing the silver goblet toward the mound north of the obelisk. Mazzah later identified the pouch as a bag of holding and confirmed that the goblet's magic was spent.

## Key Scenes / Beats

- Apparent undead hirelings attacked near the mounds.
- Sab's invisible scouting located Werner and Nabo.
- Ikram, Nabo, and Werner died in the camp confrontation.
- The note connected the spent goblet to the mound north of the obelisk.
