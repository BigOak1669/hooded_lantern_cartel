# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party finished the main exploration of the third Vakish mound, recovering Thornswild lore, a Cloak of Elvenkind, an ornate urn, and a fleeting map pointing beyond the Barrow Mounds toward Castle Zentolin. On the return to Helix they followed a lone light to a smaller mound marked with a fresh serpent-and-skull symbol, then withdrew to prepare surveillance rather than entering at night.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

Orlin forced open a swollen ceremonial door with bear-like strength. The chamber beyond preserved faded images of an elven champion and lizardfolk raiders; Orlin and Gradrick recovered the name Thornswild. Sab performed a funerary prayer, after which the preserved green shroud became or revealed itself as a magical cloak. Oogie's Trance showed a nearby chamber with an ornate urn and chaotic tablet. When Oogie read the tablet, it turned to ash and briefly displayed a much larger map of the mounds with a castle beyond them. Oogie and Grond copied what they could before it vanished; Grond then fell into a submerged spike pit and Sab healed him. The party took the urn but left the deep northern water unexplored.

Outside, the party followed a lone light and saw a large hunched figure enter a smaller mound. Its door bore a freshly painted black serpent curled around a skull, with warmth, candlelight, or incense behind it. The group chose not to enter. Back in Helix, they sold the rings and urn to Vakish, returned the second jackal, and agreed that Grond could keep the belt. Vakish named Castle Zentolin, explained the lost contact with Thornswild, and described the fog as beginning to lift. Mazzah identified Sab's shroud as a Cloak of Elvenkind, and the party retrieved the owl for surveillance.
