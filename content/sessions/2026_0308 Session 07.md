# Session Note

## Executive Summary

After a long recap of the February 22, 2026 barrow expedition, live play resumed with the party fleeing the [[location-barrow-mounds|Barrow Mounds]] at night carrying dead [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] while red-robed, steel-bone-masked figures appeared in the mist. In [[location-helix|Helix]] they chose the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] over Mazzah, used an [[item-archie-ticket|Archie ticket]] and the church cemetery compost pile to bring Oogie back, met [[npc-bartholomew|Bartholomew]], [[npc-gant|Gant]], and the hostile novice [[npc-mort|Mort]], then sold the previously recovered [[item-gold-ring|gold ring]] and [[item-four-ornate-amber-vials|four ornate amber vials]] to [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]] at the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] for 60 gold. The session ended with Oogie restored, [[pc-dern|Dern]] newly introduced to the party, [[npc-finn|Finn]] paid off, and the group buying a major carouse at the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]] that awarded 6 XP for Session 07 and pushed several characters into level-ups.

## What Happened

- The live session picked up during the retreat from the prior cliffhanger: the party was hauling dead [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] out of the prominent barrow and back toward [[location-helix|Helix]].
- During the escape, the party saw figures in red robes with steel-bone masks near the route home. At least one velvet-robed figure vanished when confronted, suggesting illusion, projection, or some other non-solid presence.
- Once back in [[location-helix|Helix]], the group chose the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] instead of Mazzah as the quickest and cheapest place to restore Oogie.
- At the [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]], [[npc-bartholomew|Bartholomew]], [[npc-gant|Gant]], and [[npc-mort|Mort]] led the party to the cemetery compost pile as the best place to regrow a fungal body. [[npc-mort|Mort]] immediately established himself as unpleasant by dumping refuse and insulting the corpse.
- The party tore [[item-archie-ticket|Oogie's Archie ticket]] and successfully brought him back. During the death-and-return sequence, Oogie asked a devil-like figure about the most powerful item in the Barrow Mounds and was told it was the "Tablet of Chaos," with the warning that power depends on the wielder.
- [[pc-dern|Dern]], a new dwarf raider, was introduced through the same devil scene and effectively dropped into the campaign as a new companion instructed to look after Oogie.
- After the resurrection, [[npc-gant|Gant]] recommended Ravos rather than [[npc-mort|Mort]] if the party wanted actual healing help.
- [[npc-finn|Finn]] directed the party to the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] to liquidate the session's loot. Vakish Baharus bought the [[item-gold-ring|gold ring]] and the four amber vials for 60 gold total and also pointed the party toward three mounds southeast of the obelisk where he hopes a fancy belt buckle may be found.
- The party paid [[npc-finn|Finn]] 8 gold as his negotiated cut, split the remainder, re-equipped Oogie as best they could, and then pooled 100 gold for a large carouse at the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]].
- The carouse roll came up high, and the GM awarded 6 XP for Session 07, with additional prior-session XP clarified at the table.

## PCs In Play

- **[[pc-oogie|Oogie]]** - Died at the end of the prior session, returned this session via a torn [[item-archie-ticket|Archie ticket]], retained half of the ticket afterward, and came back with a devil-sourced clue about the "Tablet of Chaos."
- **[[pc-dern|Dern]]** - New dwarf PC introduced during the resurrection/afterlife scene; told by the devil figure to look after Oogie and then dropped into Helix to join the group.
- **[[pc-sab|Sab]]** - Helped carry Oogie, handled the church approach, and participated in the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]] sale and the Brazen Strumpet carouse.

- **[[pc-gradrick|Gradrick]]** - Present for the retreat, Helix recovery scene, sale, and carouse.
- **[[pc-grond|Grand]]** - Carried or presented the [[item-gold-ring|gold ring]], helped with the church and sale scenes, and is still transcript-noisy as Grand/Gron/Gronn.

## NPCs Encountered

- **[[npc-finn|Finn]]** - Lantern-bearer / hireling. Helped the party get Oogie back to Helix, pointed them to the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]], collected 8 gold as his share, and sprinted off toward the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]].
- **[[npc-bartholomew|Bartholomew]]** - Fair Church brother who received the party, led them to the cemetery compost pile, accepted donations, and offered basic shelter.
- **[[npc-gant|Gant]]** - Dwarf brother of the Fair Church who handled donations and quietly advised the party to seek Ravos, not [[npc-mort|Mort]], for real healing.
- **[[npc-mort|Mort]]** - Half-orc church novice or worker who threw refuse onto the compost pile area and complained about Oogie's corpse.
- **[[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]]** - Proprietor of the [[location-rose-quartz|Rose Quartz]]. Bought the [[item-gold-ring|gold ring]] and [[item-four-ornate-amber-vials|four ornate amber vials]] for 60 gold and offered a new treasure lead involving a fancy belt buckle.
- **[[npc-steel-bone-cultist|Steel Bone cultist]] / red-robed steel-bone-masked figures** - Seen again during the retreat from the mounds; exact relationship between the single watcher and the multiple masked figures remains uncertain.

- **[[location-fair-church|Fair Church]]** - Chosen over [[npc-mazzah|Mazzah]] as the most practical place to restore Oogie; revealed to offer shelter, meals, and permissive worship space.

- **[[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]]** - Site of the party's major carouse, XP gain, and level-up conversation.

## Factions in Play

- **[[faction-steel-bone-brotherhood|Steel Bone Brotherhood]]** - Strongly implicated again by the steel-bone-masked red-robed figures seen during the retreat.
- **[[location-fair-church|Fair Church]]** - Not a hostile faction, but an important local institution newly established as a refuge, shelter, and resurrection site.

## What the Players Learned

- The [[location-fair-church|Fair Church]] can house people, feed them at least one meal per day, and function as a practical recovery site for strange bodies like Oogie's.
- [[npc-gant|Gant]] specifically recommended Ravos rather than [[npc-mort|Mort]] if the party wants competent healing help.
- [[npc-vakish-baharus|Vakish Baharus]] will buy unusual treasure, paid 60 gold for the ring and four amber vials, and wants a fancy belt buckle from one of three mounds southeast of the obelisk.
- The red-robed threat is not confined to distant sightings in the mist; red-robed steel-bone-masked figures were present near the party's route back to [[location-helix|Helix]].
- [[pc-oogie|Oogie]] came back with half of an [[item-archie-ticket|Archie ticket]] still in hand.
- Oogie learned from the devil figure that the "Tablet of Chaos" is supposedly the most powerful item in the Barrow Mounds, though the devil qualified that an item's power depends on who wields it.
- The party can convert gold into XP through large-scale carousing, and Session 07 ended with 6 XP awarded from that outcome.
