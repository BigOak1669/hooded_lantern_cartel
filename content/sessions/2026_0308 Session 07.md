## Executive Summary

The party escaped the Barrow Mounds with Oogie's body, restored him at the Fair Church through an Archie ticket, and met Dern during the same strange return sequence. They then sold the gold ring and amber perfume vials to Vakish Baharus, accepted his three-mound belt lead, and spent 100 gold on a major Brazen Strumpet carouse.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

Red-robed, steel-bone-masked figures appeared in the mist as the party fled toward Helix. At the Fair Church, Bartholomew and Gant directed the group to the cemetery compost pile as the right setting for Oogie's fungal restoration. The torn Archie ticket worked. Oogie experienced a bright interstitial encounter with the red devil, while Dern entered through the same death-return logic and was told to look after him. After Oogie returned, the party sold the ring and amber vials to Vakish at the Rose Quartz; he identified the vials as perfume and offered a lead involving a gold-and-unicorn-horn belt in one of three mounds. Finn collected his share. The party then bought out the Brazen Strumpet for a night of carousing.
