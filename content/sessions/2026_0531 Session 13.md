## Executive Summary

The party hired the young Mox as a torchbearer and explored a previously opened barrow marked by a broken sigil. Removing a cobra-headed staff from its ritual chamber brought out a mass of small snakes. Oogie found that the staff influenced them but did not stop their pursuit, so the group withdrew safely and closed the mound.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

At the Brazen Strumpet, the party hired Mox for a return to the Barrow Mounds. The chosen site had already been opened and bore a damaged painted sigil. Inside were an unopened sarcophagus, skull-filled burial alcoves, and a ritual chamber with two lizardfolk statues. A cobra-headed staff rested on a lectern or pedestal. When Oogie took it, small snakes emerged. His attempt to command or influence them changed their movement but did not make the situation safe. Grond shielded Mox during the retreat, and the party closed the stone door behind them with the staff in hand.
