# Session Note

## Executive Summary

The party resupplied at the Brazen Strumpet in Helix, hired the young Mox as a torchbearer, and returned to the Barrow Mounds. They entered a previously opened mound marked with a broken sigil, explored chambers containing a single sarcophagus, burial alcoves, and a ritual space with two lizardfolk statues. After retrieving a cobra-headed staff from a pedestal, a swarm of small snakes appeared. Oogie attempted to command them with the staff; the snakes moved but continued to follow. The group retreated safely, closed the mound door, and secured the staff for identification back in town.

## What Happened

- At the Brazen Strumpet, the inn served snails and red‑eyed brandy; the party hired Mox, a 13‑year‑old boy, as a torchbearer for the Barrow Mounds.
- The group set out for the bear mounds, using an owl to scout a mound whose door bore a broken painted sigil.
- After forcing a rusted lock, they entered the mound and discovered a room with a single sarcophagus.
- Further inside they found burial alcoves filled with skulls.
- A chamber contained two lizardfolk statues positioned as if holding up the ceiling, with a dark wooden staff topped by a cobra head resting on a lectern between them.
- When Oogie took the staff, several small snakes emerged and began slithering toward the party.
- Oogie pointed the staff at the snakes, which shifted sideways but continued to advance; the staff did not grant full control.
- Grond shielded the torchbearer, Sab stood ready with a torch, and the party retreated from the chamber while the snakes followed.
- They exited the mound and closed the stone door behind them, securing the cobra staff.
