# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party escaped a lethal dungeon expedition using offerings, careful resource management, salt, and the strange Archie-ticket return system. A portal carried them into the temple-run city of Ore and Furnace, where they rested, sold treasure, and chose Celestia over Osric as a commercial ally. Celestia's commission to acquire a silver goblet gave the group its first clear reason to travel toward Helix.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

The party recovered Orlin through the ticket system, retrieved his gear, and continued through the dungeon. At a shrine to an unidentified bovine figure, their offerings and the lit brazier produced a giant gold disc. They avoided or controlled salt-sensitive splitting undead while extracting an adamantine bar, silver, and a valuable greataxe. A portal then deposited them in an unfamiliar temple settlement. After an uneasy but safe night, they explored the market and negotiated with Osric and Celestia. The group trusted Celestia more and accepted her goblet commission.

## Key Scenes / Beats

- Orlin returned through an Archie ticket.
- The bovine shrine accepted offerings and yielded a giant gold disc.
- Salt and disciplined movement kept the splitting undead from trapping the party.
- The portal changed the campaign's surface base from the dungeon to Ore and Furnace.
- Celestia hired the party to acquire the silver goblet.
