# Session Note

<div class="quick-recap-label">Quick Read</div>

## Executive Summary

The party traveled from Ore and Furnace toward Helix for Celestia's goblet commission. A distressed dog led them to Harlan's corpse and a campsite predator, where they recovered a letter, a torn barrow map, silver, and an unknown potion. In Helix they connected Harlan, Tacey, Werner, Mazzah, and the goblet, then chose to wait until daylight before entering the Barrow Mounds.

[Jump to the detailed record →](#what-happened)

## What Happened

The party shared the road with merchant Billworth Turgin until Harlan's dog pulled them toward a swampy campsite. They killed a corpse-eating humanoid and recovered the dead traveler's belongings. In Helix, Bolo helped them identify Tacey and explained that Harlan had gone to buy her a ring. The party delivered Harlan's letter, learned that Werner had found the goblet and left it with Mazzah, and heard that Werner wanted his lost lucky sledgehammer recovered. The torn map appeared to point toward the relevant barrow. Rather than enter the mounds at night, the party took rooms at the Brazen Strumpet.

## Key Scenes / Beats

- Harlan's dog exposed the deadly roadside campsite.
- The party delivered Harlan's final letter to Tacey.
- Bolo connected Werner, Mazzah, the goblet, and the Iron Chain.
- Werner offered the sledgehammer lead in place of a simple sale.
