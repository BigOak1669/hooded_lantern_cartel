# Session Note

## Executive Summary

The party traveled from the [[location-ore-and-furnace|City of Ore and Furnace]] toward [[location-helix|Helix]] to pursue [[npc-celestia|Celestia]]’s cup-related job, now reframed less as a straightforward purchase and more as "acquiring" the item. On the road they joined a merchant named [[npc-billworth-turgin|Billworth Turgin]], followed a distressed dog to a campsite, killed a corpse-eating humanoid creature, and recovered several useful clues and valuables. After reaching Helix, they learned the sought-after chalice is tied to a drunk tomb-robber named [[npc-werner|Werner]], a local wizard named [[npc-mazzah|Maza]], and a recently disturbed barrow. They chose to rest at the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]] rather than head to the barrows at night, though they know another adventuring party may already be moving on the same lead.

## What Happened

The session opened with an in-character/out-of-character recap of prior events. The party had emerged from the dungeon into the [[location-ore-and-furnace|City of Ore and Furnace]], dealt with the [[faction-mothers-of-the-hinge|Mothers of the Hinge]], sold loot including a giant gold coin, and accepted [[npc-celestia|Celestia]]’s job to obtain a valuable goblet or chalice in [[location-helix|Helix]]. The group confirmed recent gold and experience gains, and one character arranged to have silver worked into a silver dagger on a later return.

The party set out overland for [[location-helix|Helix]]. On the road they encountered an older merchant wagon driver, [[npc-billworth-turgin|Billworth Turgin]], hauling metal goods to Helix. He allowed one party member at a time to ride and shared local context: Helix is growing because the fog is lifting from the Barrow Mounds, drawing treasure-seekers; he also mentioned rumors of a chalice priced at 666 silver pieces.

Later, a frightened dog led the party off the road into a swampy campsite. There they discovered a pale humanoid creature feeding on a dead young man. The party attacked from range and with magic, killing the creature before it could escape with the body.

At the campsite, the party recovered a crude map to a [[location-sealed-mound-on-torn-map|sealed burial mound]], a sealed letter addressed to [[npc-tacey|Tacey]], [[item-24-silver-pieces|24 silver pieces]] from the dead young man, and a small vial recovered from the monster’s stomach. Testing the vial suggested it was a [[item-potion-of-invisibility|potion of invisibility]]. The dog mourned the dead man; the party fed it and brought it along.

The party then entered [[location-helix|Helix]] at night. They noted several important buildings, but the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]] tavern was the only clearly active location. Outside, a filthy unstable man named [[npc-alistair|Alistair]] repeated phrases about "666," a "shiny cup," and a "loud man." One party member followed him and got the name "[[npc-werner|Werner]]" out of him before retreating.

Inside the [[location-brazen-strumpet|Brazen Strumpet]], the party split briefly. They identified the red-haired barmaid [[npc-tacey|Tacey]], then gave her the sealed letter recovered from the road. The letter appears to have been from [[npc-harlan|Harlan]], a young man who had been trying to reach her; her reaction strongly implied grief and personal attachment. [[npc-bolo|Bolo]], the innkeeper, explained that Harlan had gone to Ore to buy Tacey a ring.

[[npc-bolo|Bolo]] also confirmed that the loud drunk in the tavern was [[npc-werner|Werner]], who claims to have found the chalice. Werner had already handed the chalice to the wizard [[npc-mazzah|Maza]] for temporary study in exchange for gold, which explains his recent spending and drunken behavior. Werner no longer wanted cash for the chalice; instead he wanted his [[item-lucky-sledgehammer|lucky sledgehammer]] recovered from the barrow where he found it.

The party compared [[npc-werner|Werner]]’s directions with the map recovered from the campsite and concluded the sealed mound on their recovered map likely matches the same area Werner indicated. [[npc-bolo|Bolo]] also noted that another adventuring company, the [[faction-iron-chain|Iron Chain]], had been interested in the cup and had likely made some arrangement with Werner. The party debated going out immediately but decided to rent a private room, eat, drink, and rest until morning.
